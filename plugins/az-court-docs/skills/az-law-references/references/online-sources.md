# Arizona law — canonical online sources (human-facing)

> **NOT LEGAL ADVICE.** URL catalog only. Sites and paths change; confirm
> you are on the official source and that the content is current before
> relying on it.

## Statutes — Arizona Revised Statutes (A.R.S.)

- **Arizona State Legislature — A.R.S.**: `https://www.azleg.gov/arstitle/`
  - Browse by title (e.g., Title 12 Courts and Civil Proceedings; Title 25
    Marital and Domestic Relations; Title 33 Property; Title 44 Trade and
    Commerce, including the Arizona UCC enactment and consumer provisions;
    Title 47 the Uniform Commercial Code).
  - Individual section pages follow `https://www.azleg.gov/ars/<title>/<section>.htm`
    (e.g., `https://www.azleg.gov/ars/25/00211.htm` for A.R.S. § 25-211).
  - Bills and session laws: `https://www.azleg.gov/`.

## Court rules — Ariz. R. Civ. P. / Ariz. R. Evid. / ARFLP

- **Arizona Judicial Branch (azcourts.gov)**: `https://www.azcourts.gov/`
  - **Rules of court**: `https://www.azcourts.gov/rules` — the Arizona
    Rules of Civil Procedure, Arizona Rules of Evidence, Arizona Rules of
    Family Law Procedure (ARFLP), and the limited-jurisdiction court rules.
    The rules are also published in the Arizona Revised Statutes court-rule
    volumes; the azcourts.gov rules page is the authoritative free source.
  - **Rule-change / petition forum** (pending amendments): the Supreme
    Court rules-forum pages on azcourts.gov — check for pending amendments
    before relying on a rule's current text.

## Self-service, forms, and e-filing

- **Arizona Judicial Branch Self-Service Center**:
  `https://www.azcourts.gov/selfservicecenter` — statewide fillable forms
  (dissolution, family, civil, fee-deferral/waiver), instructions, and
  self-help resources for self-represented filers.
- **AZTurboCourt (statewide e-filing portal)**:
  `https://www.azturbocourt.gov/` — electronic filing and document
  assembly for participating courts. Confirm whether e-filing is mandatory
  in the filing county.
- **County Superior Court clerks** — each county clerk publishes local
  rules, fee schedules, and filing instructions (search "<county> Superior
  Court clerk Arizona"). Maricopa: `https://www.clerkofcourt.maricopa.gov/`;
  Pima: `https://www.sc.pima.gov/`.

## Appellate opinions

- **Arizona Appellate Courts opinions / memoranda**: the Arizona Judicial
  Branch opinions site on azcourts.gov publishes Arizona Supreme Court and
  Court of Appeals (Division One and Division Two) opinions and memorandum
  decisions: `https://www.azcourts.gov/opinions`.
- **Arizona Court of Appeals, Division One**:
  `https://www.azcourts.gov/coa1`.
- **Arizona Court of Appeals, Division Two**:
  `https://www.azcourts.gov/coa2`.
- **Arizona Supreme Court**: `https://www.azcourts.gov/azsupremecourt`.

## Free secondary mirrors (verify against the official source)

- **CourtListener** (Free Law Project) — Arizona Supreme Court and Court
  of Appeals opinions: `https://www.courtlistener.com/` (see
  `legal-data-apis.md` for the court identifiers and API).
- **Justia / Google Scholar** — convenient full-text search of Arizona
  opinions; treat as a finding aid, then confirm the citation and current
  status against the official opinion.
