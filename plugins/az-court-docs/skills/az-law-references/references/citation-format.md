# Arizona citation format (Bluebook-based)

> **NOT LEGAL ADVICE.** Style guidance only. When in doubt, follow the
> current edition of *The Bluebook* and any local-rule citation
> requirement of the filing court.

Arizona follows **Bluebook** citation conventions. Unlike a handful of
states with their own appellate style manual, Arizona briefs and opinions
use standard Bluebook form, with **parallel citation** to the official
Arizona Reports and the regional Pacific Reporter.

## Cases

Cite to the **official Arizona Reports** (`Ariz.`) **and** the regional
**Pacific Reporter** (`P.`, `P.2d`, `P.3d`) in parallel:

```
State v. X, 123 Ariz. 456, 789 P.2d 12 (1990)
```

- **Case name** — italicized (or underlined in typed documents): *State
  v. X*.
- **Arizona Supreme Court** — reporter is **`Ariz.`**; no court
  designation in the parenthetical (just the year): `(1990)`.
- **Arizona Court of Appeals** — historically reported as **`Ariz.
  App.`** (older reporter) and shown with a division indication for
  modern opinions; the regional parallel cite is `P.2d` / `P.3d`. Indicate
  the court in the parenthetical when not the Supreme Court — e.g.,
  `(App. 2005)` or `(Ariz. Ct. App. 2005)` per current Bluebook practice.
  Confirm the current preferred form.
- **Parallel cite order** — Arizona Reports first, then the Pacific
  Reporter: `166 Ariz. 301, 802 P.2d 1000`.
- **Pinpoint** — `123 Ariz. 456, 459, 789 P.2d 12, 15 (1990)`.

Worked example (verified):

```
Orme School v. Reeves, 166 Ariz. 301, 802 P.2d 1000 (1990)
```

## Statutes

Cite the **Arizona Revised Statutes** as **`A.R.S. § ...`** with the
section symbol:

```
A.R.S. § 12-541
A.R.S. § 12-341.01
A.R.S. § 25-211
```

- Use `§` for a single section and `§§` for a range or multiple sections.
- Subsections in parentheses: `A.R.S. § 12-341.01(A)`.

## Rules

Cite the relevant Arizona rule set by its standard abbreviation and the
rule number:

```
Ariz. R. Civ. P. 56
Ariz. R. Civ. P. 26.1
Ariz. R. Civ. P. 12(b)(6)
Ariz. R. Evid. 803(6)
Ariz. R. Evid. 902(11)
ARFLP 49                 (Arizona Rules of Family Law Procedure)
```

- The **Arizona Rules of Family Law Procedure** are commonly abbreviated
  **`ARFLP`** (some briefs write `Ariz. R. Fam. Law P.`) — confirm the
  filing court's preference; do not interchange them with the civil
  rules.

## Constitutional and other authority

- **Arizona Constitution**: `Ariz. Const. art. II, § 4`.
- **Court rules of procedure for limited-jurisdiction courts** (justice
  courts, etc.) follow their own abbreviations — confirm the correct rule
  set for the forum.

## Practical notes

- Arizona opinions issued in recent years carry a **case number / opinion
  number**; when an opinion is not yet in the bound reporters, cite the
  slip opinion or the available reporter with the appellate court and year
  in the parenthetical, and update to the parallel reporter cite when
  available.
- **Always verify a citation** against `online-sources.md` /
  `legal-data-apis.md` (CourtListener Arizona courts, the Arizona
  appellate opinions site) before relying on it.
