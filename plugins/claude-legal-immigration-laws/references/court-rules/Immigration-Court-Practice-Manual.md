## Authority note — manual vs. binding rule

This manual is **procedural guidance**, not law. The **binding** rules for immigration-court and BIA practice are the regulations at **8 CFR Part 1003** (EOIR / immigration judges / the Board), **Part 1240** (removal proceedings), and **Part 1208** (asylum before EOIR) — mirrored verbatim in [`../immigration-regulations/`](../immigration-regulations/) (`8CFR-1003-eoir-bia.md`, `8CFR-1240-removal-eoir.md`, `8CFR-1208-asylum-eoir.md`). Always cite the regulation for anything jurisdictional or substantive and use the manual only for filing format, page limits, and procedural mechanics.

## Scope

EOIR's procedural manual for practice before the immigration courts: appearances (Form EOIR-28), filing requirements and format, the master- and individual-calendar hearing structure, motions practice, deadlines, and e-filing through ECAS. Procedural guidance — for binding rules cite 8 CFR Part 1003 / 1240 / 1208 in ../immigration-regulations/.
