## Scope

Acquisition and derivation of U.S. citizenship as determined by the Department of State (birth abroad, transmission requirements, expatriation, Consular Reports of Birth Abroad).

## Note on FAM authority

The FAM is internal Department of State guidance. It is **not** binding law and does not create enforceable rights, but it is highly persuasive of the agency's interpretation of the INA and 22 CFR and is routinely cited in visa-refusal and consular-processing matters. Always pair a FAM cite with the controlling statute (INA / 8 U.S.C.) and regulation (8 CFR / 22 CFR).
