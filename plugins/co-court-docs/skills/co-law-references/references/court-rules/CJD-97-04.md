Chief Justice Directive 97-04


                        SUPREME COURT OF COLORADO
                               OFFICE OF THE CHIEF JUSTICE

                           Repeal of Chief Justice Directive 97-04


I hereby repeal Chief Justice Directive 97-04, signed May 20, 1997, concerning Court Ordered
Mental Health Evaluations - Criminal and declare it to be no longer in force or effect. The
practices in CJD 97-04 are now contained in judicial department forms JDF 31, 32, 33 and 34.


Dated at Denver, Colorado this 16th day of June, 2009.


                                                ___________/s/___________________
                                                Mary J. Mullarkey, Chief Justice
