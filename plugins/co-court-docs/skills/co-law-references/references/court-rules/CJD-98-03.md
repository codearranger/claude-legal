Chief Justice Directive 98-03


                             SUPREME COURT OF COLORADO
                                 OFFICE OF THE CHIEF JUSTICE

                              Repeal of Chief Justice Directive 98-03


I hereby repeal Chief Justice Directive 98-03, signed October 23, 2009, concerning the
preparation of the record in death penalty cases in the trial court and in the appellate courts and
declare that it be no longer in force or effect. As of the effective date of this directive, the courts
are permitted to transmit the trial court to the appellate court electronically as in all other
appeals and maintain a public access copy of the record in paper format, should they deem
necessary.


Dated at Denver, Colorado this __5th__ day of February, 2014.


                                               __________/s/____________________________
                                               Nancy E. Rice
                                               Chief Justice of the Colorado Supreme Court
