                   SUPREME COURT OF COLORADO

                    OFFICE OF THE CHIEF JUSTICE

             Repeal of Chief Justice Directive 04-01


I hereby repeal Chief Justice Directive 04-01, dated February 9,
2004, concerning the filing of disclosure materials and declare it to
be no longer in force or effect.


Dated at Denver, Colorado this   5th     day of March, 2004.


                                                   /s/
                                   Mary J. Mullarkey, Chief Justice
