Chief Justice Directive 15-01
                                                                                        Repealed


                      SUPREME COURT OF COLORADO
                              OFFICE OF THE CHIEF JUSTICE

___________________________________________________________________________

                       Repeal of Chief Justice Directive 15-01
___________________________________________________________________________

       I hereby repeal Chief Justice Directive 15-01, signed April 24, 2015, concerning Public
Access to Administrative Records of the Judicial Branch and declare it to be no longer in force
or effect. For information regarding public access to Judicial Branch administrative records,
please refer to Colorado Supreme Court Public Access to Information and Records Rule 2
(P.A.I.R.R. 2).


                                         2 day of November, 2015.
       Done at Denver, Colorado this ________


                                                         /s/
                                           ____________________________________
                                           Nancy E. Rice
                                           Chief Justice, Colorado Supreme Court
