                                                                              Directive 01-01
Amended, Effective June 2026


                           Supreme Court of Colorado
                               Office of the Chief Justice

               DIRECTIVE CONCERNING FARM WORKERS DAY


Senate Bill 01-151 and House Bill 26-1339, enacted by the General Assembly of the
State of Colorado, revise Article 11 of title 24, CRS and specify that heads of state
agencies may allow employees to have a day off with pay on Farm Workers Day, March
31, in lieu of any other legal holiday. Because the revised statute specifically prohibits
granting an additional paid holiday to state employees, state offices including the courts
will be open for business as usual on March 31.

Therefore, effective March 31, 2026 and until further order, the practice of allowing paid
time off in lieu of another state holiday in observance of Farm Workers Day is at the
discretion of the administrative authority for each judicial district/location. Such
authorities are encouraged to grant employee requests when consistent with business
needs.


                                               Done this 10th day of June, 2026

                                             Monica M. Márquez, Chief Justice
