Chief Justice Directive 85-06


                            SUPREME COURT OF COLORADO
                             OFFICE OF THE CHIEF JUSTICE


                      DIRECTIVE CONCERNING
           SOLICITATION OF FUNDS FROM PRIVATE SOURCES

No funds may be solicited or accepted from private sources for any judicial department activity
without prior approval of the chief justice.


Reissued this __16th______ day of April 2025.


                                            _________/s/________________
                                            Monica M. Márquez, Chief Justice
