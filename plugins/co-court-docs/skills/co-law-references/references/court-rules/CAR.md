> **STATUS:** Full verbatim text of the **Colorado Appellate Rules (C.A.R.)** is not published on a free Colorado-government website. The Supreme Court of Colorado adopts and amends the rule set, but the consolidated rule text is published commercially by West (Colorado Court Rules) and LexisNexis under copyright. Free third-party mirrors (Justia, FindLaw, Casetext) sit behind Cloudflare challenge pages that block automated retrieval.

> **Refresh fallback:** when this content needs to be cited verbatim in a SKILL.md body or eval, copy the rule text from the canonical source above (or the LexisNexis/West subscription publication) and replace this stub. The `pull_co_court_rules.py` refresh job does not overwrite a hand-authored MD; it only writes this stub when no file is present.

## Why no automated pull?

Appellate-court procedural rules. Published commercially by West and LexisNexis under copyright; only rule-change orders are hosted on the Colorado Judicial Branch site.

## What is available automatically

Chief Justice Directives (CJDs) — including **CJD 11-01 Statewide Electronic Filing Standards** which together with C.R.C.P. 10 defines the Colorado document-format rule — **are** auto-pulled in this same corpus.
