Chief Justice Directive 96-08


                        SUPREME COURT OF COLORADO
                               OFFICE OF THE CHIEF JUSTICE

                           Repeal of Chief Justice Directive 96-08


         I hereby repeal Chief Justice Directive 96-08, signed December 2, 1996, concerning
Processing of Dependency and Neglect Cases and declare it to be no longer in force or effect.
I find that the continued and rapid evolution of dependency and neglect law through case law and
legislation negates the overall utility of this CJD.

Dated at Denver, Colorado this 8th day of September 2023, effective immediately.


                                                ________________/s/___________
                                                Brian D. Boatright, Chief Justice
