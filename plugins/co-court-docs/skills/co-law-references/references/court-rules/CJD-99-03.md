Chief Justice Directive 99-03
                                                                                          Repealed


                        SUPREME COURT OF COLORADO
                             OFFICE OF THE CHIEF JUSTICE

  REPEAL OF CJD AUTHORIZING THE COMBINATION OF CLERKS’ OFFICES –
               DENVER DISTRICT AND JUVENILE COURTS

       I hereby repeal Chief Justice Directive 99-03, dated June 28, 1999 and effective July 1,
1999, concerning the combined administration of the Denver District and Denver Juvenile
Courts and declare it to be no longer in force or effect.


Done at Denver, Colorado this 5th day of February, 2020.


                                                          /s/
                                             Nathan B. Coats, Chief Justice
