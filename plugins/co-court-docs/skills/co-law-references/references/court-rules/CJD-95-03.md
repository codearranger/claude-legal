Chief Justice Directive 95-03
                                                                                          Repealed


                        SUPREME COURT OF COLORADO
                             OFFICE OF THE CHIEF JUSTICE

       REPEAL OF CJD CONCERNING PRIORITIES AND ASSIGNMENT FOR
              IMPLEMENTATION OF SENIOR JUDGE PROGRAM

         I hereby repeal Chief Justice Directive 95-03, dated September 19, 1995, concerning the
priorities for assignment and procedures for assignment and implementation of the senior judge
program and declare it to be no longer in force or effect. Matters concerning the Senior Judge
program will be addressed through a policy proposed by the Senior Judge Committee and
approved by the Chief Justice or the State Court Administrator.


Done at Denver, Colorado this __18th____ day of March, 2024


                                                           /s/
                                             Brian D. Boatright, Chief Justice
