Chief Justice Directive 85-27
                                                                                         Repealed


                      SUPREME COURT OF COLORADO
                              OFFICE OF THE CHIEF JUSTICE

___________________________________________________________________________

                        Repeal of Chief Justice Directive 85-27
___________________________________________________________________________

        I hereby repeal Chief Justice Directive 85-27, signed February 20, 1985, concerning
Determination of Indigency for Alcohol or Drug Treatment and declare it to be no longer in force
or effect.


                                         9 day of November, 2015.
       Done at Denver, Colorado this ________


                                                           /s/
                                            ____________________________________
                                            Nancy E. Rice
                                            Chief Justice, Colorado Supreme Court
