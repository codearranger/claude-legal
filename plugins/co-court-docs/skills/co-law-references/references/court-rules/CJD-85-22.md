Chief Justice Directive 85-22

                        SUPREME COURT OF COLORADO
                             OFFICE OF THE CHIEF JUSTICE


        RATE OF INTEREST ON JUDGMENTS WHICH ARE APPEALED


Pursuant to the statutory references cited below, the Colorado Secretary of State has
certified the following rates of interest on judgments that are appealed:

             Effective Date                 Rate                  Date Certified
            January 1, 2002                  3%                   January 1, 2002
            January 1, 2003                  3%                   January 2, 2003
            January 1, 2004                  4%                   January 2, 2004
            January 1, 2005                  5%                   January 1, 2005
            January 1, 2006                  7%                   January 3, 2006
            January 1, 2007                  8%                   January 2, 2007
            January 1, 2008                  7%                   January 7, 2008
            January 1, 2009                  3%                   January 5, 2009
            January 1, 2010                  3%                   January 4, 2010
            January 1, 2011                  3%                   January 6, 2011
            January 1, 2012                  3%                   January 3, 2012
            January 1, 2013                  3%                   January 3, 2013
            January 1, 2014                  3%                   January 6, 2014
            January 1, 2018                  4%                   January 3, 2018
            January 1, 2019                  5%                   January 8, 2019
            January 1, 2020                  4%                   January 7, 2020
            January 1, 2021                  2%                   January 5, 2021
            January 1, 2022                  2%                   January 5, 2022
            January 1, 2023                  7%                   January 5, 2023
            January 1, 2024                  8%                   January 9, 2024
            January 1, 2025                  7%                   January 8, 2025
            January 1, 2026                  6%                   January 7, 2026

APPROVED BY THE CHIEF JUSTICE

Done at Denver, Colorado, this       8th      day of January, 2026.

                                                         /s/
                                             Monica M. Márquez, Chief Justice
