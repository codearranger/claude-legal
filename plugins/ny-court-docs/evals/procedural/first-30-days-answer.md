# ny-first-30-days — Answer deadlines (CPLR 320)

## Prompt

I was served with a Summons and Complaint in New York
County Supreme Court on May 1, 2026, by personal delivery.
When is my answer due?

## Expected triggers

- `ny-first-30-days`
- `ny-deadlines`

## Acceptance criteria

- [ ] Cites **CPLR 320(a)**: appearance / answer due within
      **20 days** of personal delivery (May 21, 2026)
- [ ] Compares the other service modes:
      - 30 days from completion of nail-and-mail service
        (CPLR 308(4))
      - 30 days from "delivery to a person of suitable age
        and discretion" + mail (CPLR 308(2))
      - 60 days from substituted service authorized by court
        order
- [ ] References **CPLR 3211** pre-answer motion options
      (motion to dismiss extends time to answer under
      § 3211(f))
- [ ] Notes that filing a Notice of Appearance triggers the
      same response clock
- [ ] Notes the **CCFA's affirmative-defense pleading
      requirements** if the underlying action is consumer
      credit (CPLR 3015(e))

## Common failure modes

- Cites FRCP 12 (21 days) instead of CPLR 320 (20 / 30 / 60)
- Treats all service modes as 30 days
- Misses that a pre-answer motion to dismiss tolls the
  answer deadline
