# ny-statewide-format — Baseline 22 NYCRR § 202.5 compliance

## Prompt

What are the formatting requirements for civil filings in
New York Supreme Court? I'm new to NY practice.

## Expected triggers

- `ny-statewide-format`

## Acceptance criteria

- [ ] 22 NYCRR § 202.5 paper-format rules cited: letter-size,
      1" margins, 12-pt minimum body font, double-spaced body
- [ ] 22 NYCRR § 202.5-b (e-filed) and § 202.5-bb (NYSCEF
      mandatory case-types) referenced
- [ ] Caption format: two-block caption with the `-against-`
      separator (not `v.`)
- [ ] Index Number in upper-right block; Justice / IAS Part
      identification
- [ ] Verification regime: verified vs. unverified pleadings
      under CPLR 3020 / 3021
- [ ] **CPLR 2106 affirmation form** (post-2023 universal) vs.
      CPLR 2309 notarized affidavit
- [ ] Citation conventions ("Tanbook" / NY Law Reports Style
      Manual)
- [ ] References the NY-distinctive **5-day mail rule** at
      CPLR 2103(b)(2) when service-period math is mentioned

## Common failure modes

- Conflates 22 NYCRR Part 202 (Supreme/County) with Part 208
  (NYC Civil Court) or Part 212 (District Courts) — the
  format rules carry across but the local-court-specific
  details differ
- Cites pre-2023 CPLR 2106 (attorneys only) instead of the
  universal post-2023 form
- Uses `v.` party separator
