## What this set governs

The Commercial Division's rule set, applicable in the eight designated Commercial Division counties (New York, Kings, Bronx, Queens, Nassau, Suffolk, Westchester, Erie) plus Albany, Onondaga, and a handful of others on a per-county monetary-threshold basis. Includes the threshold table at § 202.70(a), Commercial Division-specific page limits (Rule 17 of the Appendix — 25-page motion brief default), the proportionality-in-discovery rule, and the e-discovery and accelerated-adjudication rules.
