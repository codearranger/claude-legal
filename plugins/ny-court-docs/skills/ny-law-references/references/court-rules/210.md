## What this set governs

Format and procedural rules for the ~60 upstate City Courts (Uniform City Court Act), with civil jurisdiction up to $15,000. Major examples: Buffalo, Rochester, Syracuse, Albany, Yonkers, White Plains.

interactive HTML; no login is required.
