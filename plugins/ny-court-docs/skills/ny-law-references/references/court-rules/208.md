## What this set governs

Format and procedural rules for the Civil Court of the City of New York — the separate trial court (Civil Court Act) handling claims up to $50,000 and small-claims matters up to $10,000 in five boroughs. The primary forum for consumer-debt collection litigation in NYC. Includes the 2022 CCFA default-judgment companion rule at § 208.6-a.
