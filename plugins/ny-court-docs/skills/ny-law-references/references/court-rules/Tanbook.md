## What this set governs

The official citation manual for New York court papers, published by the Reporter of Decisions. The 'Tanbook' controls citation form for New York cases, statutes, and regulations in trial-court filings. Not part of 22 NYCRR; downloaded as a PDF from the Reporter's site.
