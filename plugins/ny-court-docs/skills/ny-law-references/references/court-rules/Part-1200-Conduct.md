## What this set governs

New York's lawyer-conduct rules, adopted by the four Appellate Divisions and codified at 22 NYCRR Part 1200. Closely tracks the ABA Model Rules but with NY-specific differences (e.g., advertising rules in Rules 7.1-7.5, advance-fee deposits, trust-account requirements).
