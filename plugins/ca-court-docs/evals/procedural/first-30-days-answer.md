# ca-first-30-days — Response options within 30 days

## Prompt

I was just served with a complaint in LASC. What are my response
options and deadlines?

## Expected triggers

- `ca-first-30-days`
- `ca-deadlines`

## Acceptance criteria

### Response window

- [ ] 30 days from completed service (CCP § 412.20(a)(3))
- [ ] Note for service by mail with NORF (CCP § 415.30)

### Response options

- [ ] **Answer** — CCP § 431.30 (general denials + affirmative
      defenses)
- [ ] **Demurrer (general)** — CCP § 430.10(e) failure to state
      cause of action; § 430.10(f) uncertain
- [ ] **Motion to Strike** — CCP § 435 (irrelevant, false,
      improper matter)
- [ ] **Anti-SLAPP Motion** — CCP § 425.16 (60-day window)
- [ ] **Motion to Quash Service** — CCP § 418.10
- [ ] **Cross-Complaint** — CCP §§ 426.10-426.50 (compulsory
      if related)

### Meet-and-confer requirements

- [ ] Demurrer — CCP § 430.41 (5 days before filing)
- [ ] Motion to Strike — CCP § 435.5 (5 days before filing)

### Affirmative defenses (in answer)

- [ ] Must be pleaded with facts (general denials by paragraph
      not allowed against verified complaints — CCP § 431.30)
- [ ] Common defenses: SOL, accord and satisfaction, estoppel,
      laches, payment, release

### Default risk

- [ ] Default may be entered if no response within 30 days
      (CCP § 585)

## Common failure modes

- Forgetting the meet-and-confer requirements (CA-specific —
  not in WA/OR)
- Confusing demurrer with motion to dismiss (federal
  terminology)
- Missing the 60-day anti-SLAPP window
- Importing OR's ORCP 21 motion-to-dismiss triage instead of
  CCP § 430.10 demurrer
- Importing WA's CR 12(b)(6) instead of CCP § 430.10(e)
