# Integration — End-to-end debt-defense answer

## Prompt

I just got served with a complaint in LASC by "Cavalry SPV I,
LLC" for $5,400 on a Bank of America credit card I haven't paid
since November 2021. The complaint was filed March 1, 2026. I'm
pro per. Walk me through everything I need to do in the next 30
days and draft my answer.

## Expected triggers

- `ca-first-30-days`
- `ca-consumer-debt`
- `ca-deadlines`
- `ca-pro-se`
- `ca-statewide-format`
- `ca-lasc`
- `ca-draft-motion` or `ca-draft-declaration` (potentially)
- `ca-file-packet`

## Acceptance criteria

### Triage

- [ ] Identifies as debt-buyer case (Pattern 2 of 5)
- [ ] Flags FDBPA pleading requirements
- [ ] Notes CDCLA license-status check
- [ ] SOL analysis: last payment Nov 2021 → SOL ~ Nov 2025 →
      complaint Mar 2026 → POTENTIALLY TIME-BARRED (CCP § 337,
      4-year)

### 30-day calendar

- [ ] Response due 30 days from service (CCP § 412.20(a)(3))
- [ ] Concrete deadline calculation
- [ ] Holiday adjustments per CCP § 12a (e.g., Cesar Chavez
      Day on Mar 31 if applicable; weekend rule)

### Response strategy

- [ ] Demurrer option (CCP § 430.10(e) — fails to state cause
      due to FDBPA pleading deficiencies; CCP § 430.10(e) —
      SOL apparent on face)
- [ ] Answer option (general denials + affirmative defenses)
- [ ] Cross-complaint option (Rosenthal Act / UCL)
- [ ] Meet-and-confer requirement if demurrer (CCP § 430.41)

### Answer draft

- [ ] CRC 2.111 caption with LASC header
- [ ] General denial (not allowed for verified complaint
      paragraph-by-paragraph)
- [ ] **Affirmative defenses (minimum 8-12)**:
  - SOL (CCP § 337)
  - Failure to state cause (FDBPA § 1788.58 deficiencies)
  - Lack of standing
  - Want of consideration
  - Payment
  - Release
  - Estoppel
  - Laches
  - Unclean hands
  - CDCLA non-licensure (Fin. Code § 100001)
  - Rosenthal Act violation
  - UCL violation

### Pro per conventions

- [ ] "In Pro Per" in signature block
- [ ] No bar number
- [ ] pro-se drafting framework adapted for pro per

### Filing logistics

- [ ] LASC e-filing via Odyssey eFileCA
- [ ] Filing fee analysis (~$435 for first appearance) +
      fee-waiver options (FW-001 / FW-003)
- [ ] Proof of Service requirements (POS-040 / POS-030)

### Discovery roadmap

- [ ] Initial RFPs targeting chain of title
- [ ] Form interrogatories (DISC-001 General)
- [ ] Up to 35 special interrogatories (CCP § 2030.030)
- [ ] RFAs (CCP § 2033)

## Common failure modes

- Missing the SOL analysis (4-year on written contract)
- Importing Oregon's ORS 12.080 6-year SOL
- Failing to surface FDBPA-specific pleading defenses
- Missing the CDCLA licensure check
- Missing the 60-day anti-SLAPP window (if applicable; not
  here)
- Combining Notice of Motion + Memo into one document
- Citing FRCP / federal rules
- Calling response a "motion to dismiss" (CA uses "demurrer")
- Including bar number for pro per filer
