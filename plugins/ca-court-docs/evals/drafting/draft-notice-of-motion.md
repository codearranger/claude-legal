# ca-draft-note — Notice of Motion scaffold

## Prompt

I have a motion to compel further responses to RFPs ready to file
in LASC. I've reserved a hearing date for July 15, 2026 at 8:30
a.m. in Dept. 26. Draft the Notice of Motion.

## Expected triggers

- `ca-draft-note`
- `ca-statewide-format`
- `ca-lasc`

## Acceptance criteria

### Document identity

- [ ] Separate document from the Memorandum of P&A (California
      convention; not combined)
- [ ] Title: "NOTICE OF MOTION AND MOTION TO COMPEL FURTHER
      RESPONSES TO REQUESTS FOR PRODUCTION (SET ONE)"

### Required content (CCP § 1010)

- [ ] Date of hearing: July 15, 2026
- [ ] Time of hearing: 8:30 a.m.
- [ ] Department: Dept. 26
- [ ] Courthouse: Stanley Mosk Courthouse, 111 N. Hill St., Los
      Angeles
- [ ] Nature of motion (relief sought)
- [ ] Grounds for motion (with CCP §§ 2031.310 citation)
- [ ] Statement of supporting papers ("based on this Notice, the
      attached Memorandum of Points and Authorities, the
      Separate Statement, the Declaration of [Name], the
      pleadings on file, and such oral argument as may be
      received at the hearing")

### Service / timing

- [ ] References the 16-court-day notice requirement under CCP
      § 1005(b)
- [ ] Backs into a service deadline from the July 15 hearing
- [ ] Notes addition for mail / e-service if applicable (5 cal
      days mail / 2 court days e-service)

### Pro se conventions

- [ ] Caption matches CRC 2.111
- [ ] Pro per filer designation in attorney-info block

## Common failure modes

- Combining Notice of Motion + Memo into one document (CA
  separates them)
- Calling it a "Notice of Hearing" (Oregon usage) or a "Note for
  Motion Docket" (Washington usage)
- Missing the 16-court-day backstop
- Failing to specify the department
- Not using the "based on this Notice, ..." formula
