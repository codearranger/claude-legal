# id-pro-se — self-represented drafting conventions

## Prompt

I'm representing myself in an Idaho civil case. How should I sign and
identify myself on my documents, and is there anything I should know
about e-filing as a self-represented person?

## Expected triggers

- `id-pro-se`
- `id-statewide-format`

## Acceptance criteria

- [ ] Identifies the filer as **self-represented** in the signature
      block and **omits the Idaho State Bar number** (which only
      licensed attorneys carry)
- [ ] States the signature block must include the signer's address,
      email, and telephone number (I.R.C.P. 11)
- [ ] Explains that self-represented individuals **may elect** to
      e-file through iCourt but are **not required** to (I.R.E.F.S.
      4(b)), and flags that once they opt in they are generally bound
      to e-file for the life of the case
- [ ] Points to Idaho self-help resources (Idaho Court Assistance
      Office / Odyssey "Guide & File") for forms
- [ ] Adds the consult-an-attorney prompt for high-stakes matters

## Common failure modes

- Tells a pro se filer to include a bar number
- Says e-filing is mandatory for self-represented filers
- Omits the opt-in lock-in consequence
