# id-draft-declaration — declaration or affidavit

## Prompt

I need to put my own facts in front of an Idaho court to support my
motion. Do I need a notary, or can I just sign something?

## Expected triggers

- `id-draft-declaration`

## Acceptance criteria

- [ ] Explains that Idaho accepts an **unsworn declaration under
      penalty of perjury** (Idaho Code § 9-1406) as an alternative to a
      notarized **Affidavit**, and that both can support a motion under
      I.R.C.P. 56(c)
- [ ] Produces the correct subscription language for an unsworn
      declaration (declaring under penalty of perjury under the laws of
      the State of Idaho, with date and signature)
- [ ] Identifies the user as the **declarant** who chooses and swears
      to the facts
- [ ] Limits the declaration to admissible, personal-knowledge facts
      (not argument) and applies the I.R.C.P. 2 caption/format

## Common failure modes

- Says a notarized affidavit is the only option
- Omits "under the laws of the State of Idaho" from the declaration
- Fills the declaration with legal argument
