# id-draft-note — Notice of Hearing

## Prompt

How do I get my motion in front of an Idaho judge — what's the
scheduling document called and how much notice do I give?

## Expected triggers

- `id-draft-note`
- `id-schedule-hearing`

## Acceptance criteria

- [ ] Identifies Idaho's scheduling document as the **Notice of
      Hearing** (not a "note for motion" or other terminology)
- [ ] States the notice must be served and filed at least **14 days**
      before the hearing under **I.R.C.P. 7(b)(3)**, and flags the
      longer summary-judgment service window where applicable
- [ ] Produces a Notice of Hearing with caption, the motion being
      heard, the date/time/place (or remote link), and a certificate
      of service
- [ ] Routes the date-reservation step to `id-schedule-hearing` /
      the venue skills

## Common failure modes

- Uses non-Idaho scheduling terminology
- Gives the wrong notice period
