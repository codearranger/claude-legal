# Idaho Answer — Skeleton (I.R.C.P. 8)

> **NOT LEGAL ADVICE.** A drafting scaffold only; verify every rule and
> deadline against current Idaho law before filing.

A self-represented defendant's answer responds **paragraph-by-paragraph**
to the complaint (admit / deny / lack knowledge under I.R.C.P. 8(b)),
states every **affirmative defense** (I.R.C.P. 8(c) — omission may waive),
and asserts any **counterclaim** (I.R.C.P. 13). Serve and file within
**21 days after service of the summons and complaint** (I.R.C.P.
12(a)(1)(A); +3 days where the period runs from service by mail). Defer
the full caption / margin / font recipe to `id-statewide-format`; the
block below is the procedural skeleton only.

## Body

```
   COMES NOW Defendant [Name], appearing pro se, and answers the
   Complaint of Plaintiff as follows:

                              ANSWER

   1. Defendant admits the allegations of paragraph 1.
   2. Defendant denies the allegations of paragraph 2.
   3. Defendant lacks knowledge or information sufficient to form a
      belief about the truth of the allegations of paragraph 3, and on
      that basis denies them (I.R.C.P. 8(b)).
   4. Defendant admits the allegations of the first sentence of
      paragraph 4 and denies the remainder.
   [... one numbered response for EVERY numbered paragraph of the
   complaint; an allegation not addressed may be deemed admitted, so
   respond to each ...]

   GENERAL DENIAL. Except as expressly admitted above, Defendant
   denies each and every allegation of the Complaint.

                       AFFIRMATIVE DEFENSES
                (I.R.C.P. 8(c) — omission may WAIVE)

   FIRST AFFIRMATIVE DEFENSE. Plaintiff's claims are barred in whole
   or in part by the applicable statute of limitations.

   SECOND AFFIRMATIVE DEFENSE. Plaintiff lacks standing and has not
   established the chain of title to the alleged debt.

   THIRD AFFIRMATIVE DEFENSE. [Payment / accord and satisfaction /
   release / discharge in bankruptcy / statute of frauds / failure of
   consideration / estoppel / waiver / laches / res judicata / failure
   to state a claim — plead each one that may apply; see
   `affirmative-defense-catalog.md`.]

   RESERVATION. Defendant reserves the right to assert additional
   affirmative defenses as discovery proceeds.

                            COUNTERCLAIM
                       (I.R.C.P. 13, if any)

   [Plead complaint-style: numbered factual allegations, then named
   counts. A compulsory counterclaim — one arising from the same
   transaction or occurrence — may be BARRED if omitted (Rule 13(a)).]

                          PRAYER FOR RELIEF

   WHEREFORE, Defendant prays that the Complaint be dismissed with
   prejudice, that Plaintiff take nothing, that Defendant recover costs
   and attorney fees as allowed by law (Idaho Code §§ 12-120, 12-121),
   and for such other relief as the Court deems just.
```

## Signature block (self-represented)

```
   DATED this ____ day of ____________, 20____.


                                   _______________________________
                                   [Defendant Name], Pro Se
                                   [Address / Phone / Email]
```

> An attorney's block would add the **Idaho State Bar number**; a
> self-represented filer omits it. Signing certifies the pleading under
> **I.R.C.P. 11**.
