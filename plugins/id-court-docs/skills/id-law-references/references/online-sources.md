# Idaho law — canonical online sources (human-facing)

> **NOT LEGAL ADVICE.** URL catalog only. Sites and paths change; confirm you
> are on the official source and that the content is current before relying on
> it.

## Statutes — Idaho Code

- **Idaho Legislature — Idaho Code**:
  `https://legislature.idaho.gov/statutesrules/idstat/`
  - Browse by title (e.g., Title 5 Limitation of Actions; Title 11
    Enforcement of Judgments / Exemptions; Title 12 Costs and Attorney Fees;
    Title 28 Commercial Transactions / Idaho UCC and Idaho Credit Code; Title
    32 Domestic Relations; Title 48 Consumer Protection; Title 55 Property and
    Homestead; Title 73 General Provisions / Holidays).
  - Individual section pages follow
    `https://legislature.idaho.gov/statutesrules/idstat/title<title>/t<title>ch<chapter>/sect<section>/`
    — e.g., `.../Title5/T5CH2/SECT5-216/` for I.C. § 5-216.
  - Idaho Session Laws and bill history: `https://legislature.idaho.gov/`.

## Court rules — I.R.C.P. / I.R.E. / I.R.F.L.P. / I.A.R. / I.R.E.F.S.

- **Idaho Supreme Court (isc.idaho.gov) — Rules of Procedure**:
  `https://isc.idaho.gov/rules-procedure/`
  - **Idaho Rules of Civil Procedure (I.R.C.P.)** — per-rule print pages at
    `https://isc.idaho.gov/rules-procedure/print/ircp/<n>` and the "new"
    pages at `https://isc.idaho.gov/ircp<n>-new`.
  - **Idaho Rules of Evidence (I.R.E.)**:
    `https://isc.idaho.gov/rules-procedure/ire`.
  - **Idaho Rules of Family Law Procedure (I.R.F.L.P.)**:
    `https://isc.idaho.gov/rules-procedure/irfl`.
  - **Idaho Rules for Electronic Filing and Service (I.R.E.F.S.)**:
    `https://isc.idaho.gov/rules-procedure/irefs`.
  - **Idaho Appellate Rules (I.A.R.)** and amendment / pending-order pages are
    on the same rules-procedure site — check for pending amendments before
    relying on a rule's current text.

## Self-service, forms, and e-filing

- **Idaho Court Assistance Office / Self-Help**: the Idaho Judicial Branch
  self-help and court-forms site (`https://icourt.idaho.gov/`) publishes
  statewide fillable forms (divorce, family, civil, fee-waiver), instructions,
  and self-help resources for self-represented filers.
- **iCourt e-filing — Odyssey File & Serve**: Idaho's statewide electronic
  filing portal (Odyssey File & Serve), governed by the **I.R.E.F.S.**
  E-filing is **mandatory for attorneys** and **optional for self-represented
  parties**. Reach the portal through `https://icourt.idaho.gov/`.
- **County District Court clerks** — each county clerk publishes local
  filing instructions and fee information; search "<county> District Court
  clerk Idaho."

## Appellate opinions

- **Idaho Supreme Court & Court of Appeals opinions**: published on the Idaho
  Supreme Court site, `https://isc.idaho.gov/` (appellate-opinions /
  decisions pages). Covers the Idaho Supreme Court and the Idaho Court of
  Appeals.

## Regulators

- **Idaho Department of Finance**: `https://www.finance.idaho.gov/` —
  administers the Idaho Collection Agency Act (I.C. § 26-2222 et seq.) and the
  Idaho Credit Code; licensee lookups and consumer-finance regulatory
  guidance.

## Free secondary mirrors (verify against the official source)

- **CourtListener** (Free Law Project) — Idaho Supreme Court and Court of
  Appeals opinions: `https://www.courtlistener.com/` (see `legal-data-apis.md`
  for the court identifiers and API).
- **Justia / Google Scholar** — convenient full-text search of Idaho
  opinions; treat as a finding aid, then confirm the citation and current
  status against the official opinion.
