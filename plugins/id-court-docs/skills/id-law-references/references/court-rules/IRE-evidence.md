## Scope

The Idaho Rules of Evidence (modeled on the Federal Rules of Evidence, sharing
the three-digit numbering) govern admissibility in Idaho civil and criminal
proceedings: relevance (Rules 401-403), hearsay and its exceptions (Rules
801-807, including the **business-records exception Rule 803(6)**),
authentication and self-authentication (Rules 901-902, including **certified
business records under Rule 902(11)** with its custodian-certification and
pre-trial notice / inspection requirements), and privileges. A navigational
digest is in `../evidence-rules.md`.
