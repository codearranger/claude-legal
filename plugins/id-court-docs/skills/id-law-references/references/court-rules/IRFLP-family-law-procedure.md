## Scope

The Idaho Rules of Family Law Procedure are a **separate rule set** governing
family-law cases in the **Magistrate Division** of the District Court: scope
(**Rule 101** — annulment, divorce, legal separation, separate maintenance,
child support, custody, grandparent visitation, paternity), the **Idaho Child
Support Guidelines** (**Rule 120**, income-shares model), and **form of
documents** (**Rule 208**). Do not cite an I.R.C.P. number in a family matter.
Substantive family law is in Idaho Code Title 32. A navigational digest is in
`../family-rules.md`.
