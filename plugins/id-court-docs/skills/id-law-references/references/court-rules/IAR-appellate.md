## Scope

The Idaho Appellate Rules govern appeals to the Idaho Supreme Court and Idaho
Court of Appeals: the **time for appeal (Rule 14 — generally 42 days** from the
judgment or order), **cross-appeals (Rule 15)**, the contents of the notice of
appeal, the record and transcript, briefing, and oral argument. Confirm the
current deadlines and any tolling post-trial motions before relying. A
navigational summary is in `../civil-rules.md` (Appeal section).
