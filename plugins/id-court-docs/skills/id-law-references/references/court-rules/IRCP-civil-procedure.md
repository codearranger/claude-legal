## Scope

The Idaho Rules of Civil Procedure govern general civil practice in the
District Court and Magistrate Division: document form and caption (Rule 2),
pleadings and signing (Rules 10, 11), **time computation (Rule 2.2 — Rule 6 is
RESERVED)**, service (Rules 4 and 5), responsive pleadings and the 21-day
answer (Rule 12), discovery (Rules 26-37, including the 40-interrogatory cap
under Rule 33), summary judgment (Rule 56), default (Rule 55), judgments and
the 14-day memorandum of costs (Rule 54), new trial / alter-or-amend (Rule 59),
reconsideration (Rule 11.2), and relief from judgment (Rule 60). A navigational
digest is in `../civil-rules.md`.
