# Notice of Hearing — I.R.C.P. 7(b)(3)

> **NOT LEGAL ADVICE.** Drafting aid only. Verify the 14-day lead time,
> the assigned judge's setting procedure, and any local rule against
> the current rule text before serving.

A motion is not self-executing — to get it heard you serve a **written
motion and a Notice of Hearing**. Pull the controlling text from
`../../id-law-references/references/court-rules/IRCP-civil-procedure.md`
(Rule 7(b); Rule 56 for summary judgment).

## The 14-day rule

Under **I.R.C.P. 7(b)(3)**, a written motion and notice of the hearing
must be **served at least 14 days before the time set for the hearing**,
unless the rule or a court order shortens the time. Confirm the exact
day count and any shortened-time mechanism against the corpus — do not
rely on memory for the drift-prone interval.

- Compute the date with `id-deadlines` (I.R.C.P. 2.2 time computation).
- Add the **+3-day mail add-on** when the period runs from service by
  mail.
- **Summary-judgment motions run longer** under **I.R.C.P. 56**: the
  motion and supporting brief are served at least **28 days** before
  the hearing and the answering brief at least **14 days** before the
  hearing. Verify the Rule 56 windows in the corpus.

## Reserve the date first

The hearing date is typically coordinated with the court or the
assigned judge's clerk **before** the Notice of Hearing issues. See
`id-schedule-hearing`. Confirm the **District Court** vs. **Magistrate
Division** routing — the caption and the assigned judge title differ.

## Notice of Hearing scaffold

```
                       NOTICE OF HEARING

   TO: [Opposing party / counsel]

   PLEASE TAKE NOTICE that [movant] will bring the foregoing
   [Motion to ___] on for hearing before the above-entitled Court at
   the [County] County Courthouse, [address], on [DATE] at [TIME],
   or as soon thereafter as the matter may be heard. [If remote: The
   hearing will be conducted by [Zoom / telephone]; see the court's
   current remote-appearance instructions.]

   DATED this ___ day of __________, 20__.

                                   ___________________________
                                   [Name]
                                   [ISB No. ___ / Self-Represented]
```

Attach a **Certificate of Service (I.R.C.P. 5)** showing the manner,
date, and recipients. The notice's hearing date must respect the
14-day (or Rule 56 28-day) lead measured from the date of service.
