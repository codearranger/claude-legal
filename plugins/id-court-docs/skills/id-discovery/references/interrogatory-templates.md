# Interrogatory Templates — I.R.C.P. 33

> **NOT LEGAL ADVICE.** These are drafting scaffolds. Tailor them to the
> claims and verify the current rule text and numeric cap in the corpus
> before serving.

Written interrogatories to a party are governed by **I.R.C.P. 33**.

- **40-cap, including subparts.** Under **I.R.C.P. 33(a)(1)** a party may
  serve **no more than 40 interrogatories, counting each discrete subpart
  as a separate interrogatory**, without leave of court or a written
  stipulation. To exceed the cap, obtain a stipulation or move for leave.
- **Response window — 30 days.** Answers are served separately and fully,
  in writing **under oath**, within **30 days** (I.R.C.P. 33(b)(2)).

Verbatim rule text:
`../../id-law-references/references/court-rules/IRCP-civil-procedure.md`.
Use the I.R.C.P. 2 caption (defer the full recipe to `id-statewide-format`)
and end with a certificate of service.

## Preamble

```
[CAPTION — per id-statewide-format]

PLAINTIFF'S FIRST SET OF INTERROGATORIES TO DEFENDANT

Pursuant to I.R.C.P. 33, [Plaintiff] requests that [Defendant] answer the
following Interrogatories separately and fully, in writing under oath,
within 30 days of service. These Interrogatories are continuing; supplement
your answers as required by I.R.C.P. 26(e).
```

## Instructions and definitions (abbreviated)

```
DEFINITIONS
  "You" / "Your" means [party] and its agents, employees, and attorneys.
  "Document" has the meaning given in I.R.C.P. 34(a).
  "Identify," as to a person, means state the full name, last known
  address, and telephone number.

INSTRUCTIONS
  If you withhold any responsive information on a claim of privilege,
  state the privilege and provide a log sufficient to assess the claim
  (I.R.C.P. 26(b)(5)).
```

## Sample interrogatories (count subparts toward the 40-cap)

```
INTERROGATORY NO. 1: Identify each person known to you to have knowledge
of the facts alleged in the [Complaint / Answer], and describe the subject
matter of that knowledge.

INTERROGATORY NO. 2: Identify each person you may call as a witness at
trial, and state the substance of the testimony you expect from each.

INTERROGATORY NO. 3: State the factual basis for [each affirmative defense
asserted in your Answer / your contention that ___], identifying the
documents and witnesses supporting it.

INTERROGATORY NO. 4: Identify each document you contend supports [the
claim / defense stated in ___], by date, author, recipient, and subject.

INTERROGATORY NO. 5: If you contend [specific fact], state each fact on
which that contention rests.
```

> Each "Identify each person AND state the substance / AND describe ..."
> phrasing can be read as multiple discrete subparts. Track the running
> subpart count against the **40-cap (I.R.C.P. 33(a)(1))**.

## Answer block (when responding)

```
ANSWER TO INTERROGATORY NO. 1: [Full narrative answer, under oath.]

OBJECTION TO INTERROGATORY NO. 2: [State the specific ground — e.g., the
interrogatory is not proportional to the needs of the case under I.R.C.P.
26(b)(1) because ___. Subject to and without waiving the objection, ___.]
```

Where the answer may be derived from business records, consider production
in lieu of a narrative answer on the conditions of **I.R.C.P. 33(d)**.

End with a verification under oath and a certificate of service.
