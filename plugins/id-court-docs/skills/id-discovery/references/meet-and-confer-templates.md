# Meet-and-Confer Templates

> **NOT LEGAL ADVICE.** Drafting scaffolds only. Confirm the venue's
> specific conferral requirement in the corpus before filing any discovery
> motion.

Before filing any discovery motion, **confer in good faith** with the
opposing party and **document** the attempt. Idaho conditions discovery
relief under **I.R.C.P. 37** on a good-faith effort to resolve the dispute
without court action, and the motion must include a **certification** that
the effort was made. Confirm the venue's specific requirement in
`../../id-law-references/references/court-rules/IRCP-civil-procedure.md`.

## Letter / email — first conferral

```
Subject: Meet-and-Confer re: Discovery Responses —
         [Case Short Title], Case No. [Number]

[Name],

This is my good-faith effort to confer under I.R.C.P. 37 before I file a
Motion to Compel. Your responses to my [First Set of Interrogatories /
First Requests for Production / First Requests for Admission], served on
[DATE], are deficient as follows:

  1. Interrogatory No. 3 — Boilerplate objection that does not specify the
     allegedly vague term or quantify the claimed burden.
  2. Request for Production No. 7 — No documents produced, though the
     [Complaint / Answer] references documents within this request.
  3. Request for Admission No. 2 — Evasive non-answer that does not fairly
     meet the substance of the request.

Please supplement by [date]. I am available to confer by telephone on
[dates/times]. If I do not hear from you, I will proceed with a Motion to
Compel and seek the reasonable expenses caused by the failure under
I.R.C.P. 37.

[Name]
[Contact information]
```

## Follow-up confirming the conferral occurred

```
Subject: Confirming our meet-and-confer — [Case Short Title], Case No. [Number]

[Name],

This confirms our [telephone conference / correspondence] of [date]
concerning the discovery disputes identified in my [date] letter. We
[resolved / did not resolve] the following:

  - [Item] — [resolved: defendant to supplement by ___ / unresolved].
  - [Item] — [resolved / unresolved].

For any item that remains unresolved by [date], I will move to compel and
will attach this correspondence as the certification of good-faith
conferral required by I.R.C.P. 37.

[Name]
```

## Conferral certification (for use in the motion)

```
CERTIFICATION OF GOOD-FAITH CONFERRAL

I certify that, before filing this Motion, I conferred in good faith with
[opposing party / counsel] in an effort to obtain the discovery without
court action, as required by I.R.C.P. 37. My efforts are described in the
accompanying [Affidavit / Declaration] and the attached correspondence
(Exhibits ___). The parties were unable to resolve the disputes addressed
in this Motion.

                                            /s/ [Name]
```

Keep dated copies of all conferral correspondence; attach them as exhibits
to the motion to compel — see `motion-to-compel.md`.
