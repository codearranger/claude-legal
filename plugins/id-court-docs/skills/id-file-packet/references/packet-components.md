# Motion Packet Components

> **NOT LEGAL ADVICE.** A component checklist. Confirm the required
> documents and the document-type labels with the filing court before
> submitting.

A standard Idaho trial-court motion packet consists of these documents.
Pull the controlling rule text from
`../../id-law-references/references/court-rules/IRCP-civil-procedure.md`.

| # | Document | Authority / note |
|---|----------|------------------|
| 1 | **Motion** | I.R.C.P. 7(b) — states the relief sought and the grounds **with particularity** |
| 2 | **Memorandum in support** | The supporting brief, presented with the motion |
| 3 | **Notice of Hearing** | I.R.C.P. 7(b)(3) — served/filed **≥ 14 days** before the hearing (≥ 28 days for a Rule 56 motion) |
| 4 | **Affidavit or Declaration** | Notarized affidavit **or** an Idaho Code § 9-1406 declaration under penalty of perjury; business-records foundation under I.R.E. 803(6) |
| 5 | **Exhibits** | Written instruments referenced in the motion — part of the pleading under I.R.C.P. 10 |
| 6 | **Proposed order** | A **separate document** for the judge's signature; see `id-submit-order` |
| 7 | **Certificate of Service** | I.R.C.P. 5 — date, manner, recipients |

## Notes on the components

- **Motion + Memorandum** travel together; the motion states relief and
  grounds, the Memorandum argues them.
- **Affidavit vs. declaration** — an Idaho Code § 9-1406 declaration
  ("under penalty of perjury under the laws of the State of Idaho")
  carries the same weight as a notarized affidavit without a notary. An
  un-notarized "affidavit" lacking the § 9-1406 subscription has no
  evidentiary weight.
- **Proposed order** is always a **separate document**; a final
  **Judgment** is a separate document stating only relief (I.R.C.P.
  54(a)). See `references` under `id-submit-order`.
- **Notice of Hearing timing** is the most common defect — confirm the
  14-day (or Rule 56 28-day) lead with the mail add-on via
  `id-deadlines`.

## Certificate of Service (I.R.C.P. 5)

```
                    CERTIFICATE OF SERVICE

   I certify that on [DATE] I served the foregoing [document title]
   on the following, by the method indicated:

   [Name / counsel]   [ ] iCourt e-service (I.R.E.F.S. 17)
   [address]          [ ] U.S. Mail (+3 days)  [ ] hand  [ ] email

                                   ___________________________
                                   [Name]
                                   [ISB No. ___ / Self-Represented]
```

The certificate-of-service date should match the signature date on the
filing; the recipients and manner must be accurate for each party.
