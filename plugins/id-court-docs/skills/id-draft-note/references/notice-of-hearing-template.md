# Idaho Notice of Hearing Template (I.R.C.P. 7(b)(3))

> **NOT LEGAL ADVICE.** This is a fill-in-the-blank drafting aid. Verify the
> filing court's local rules and the assigned judge's calendaring practice
> before filing.

In Idaho civil practice, a motion is set for hearing by a **Notice of
Hearing** under **I.R.C.P. 7(b)(3)**: the moving party files and serves it,
and the notice and motion must be **served and filed at least 14 days
before the hearing** (longer for a Rule 56 motion — see
`notice-period.md`). The full caption recipe and line numbering come from
`id-statewide-format` (I.R.C.P. 2). Verbatim rule text:
`../../id-law-references/references/court-rules/IRCP-civil-procedure.md`.

## Certificate of Service (I.R.C.P. 5)

The Notice of Hearing is served on every other party; a certificate of
service is required for papers served after the complaint.

```
                  CERTIFICATE OF SERVICE

   I certify that on [date] I served a true and correct copy of
the foregoing on the following, by [iCourt e-service / U.S. mail,
postage prepaid / hand delivery]:

   [Name and address of each party/attorney served]

                                  ____________________________
                                  [Name]
```

## Notes

- Obtain or coordinate the hearing date with the court before preparing the
  Notice — see `id-schedule-hearing` and the venue overlay.
- Title `NOTICE OF HEARING` in ALL CAPS, centered.
- Compute the 14-day (or 28-day) count and any mail add-on with
  `id-deadlines`; the day-count rules are in `notice-period.md`.
