# Idaho Motion + Supporting Memorandum Template (I.R.C.P. 7(b))

> **NOT LEGAL ADVICE.** This is a fill-in-the-blank drafting aid. Verify
> every rule, deadline, and citation against the current Idaho Rules of
> Civil Procedure before filing.

A written motion is governed by **I.R.C.P. 7(b)**: it must be in writing,
state with particularity the grounds, and state the relief sought
(I.R.C.P. 7(b)(1)). It is presented with a **supporting Memorandum** (the
brief). The full caption recipe, line numbering, margins, font, and the
"title at the foot of each page" requirement come from `id-statewide-format`
(I.R.C.P. 2) — do not re-embed them here. Verbatim rule text:
`../../id-law-references/references/court-rules/IRCP-civil-procedure.md`.

## The Motion

```
       [Caption — title of court, parties, case number; see id-statewide-format]

IN THE DISTRICT COURT OF THE [____] JUDICIAL DISTRICT OF THE
STATE OF IDAHO, IN AND FOR THE COUNTY OF [____]
[or: MAGISTRATE DIVISION]

[PLAINTIFF NAME],                      )  Case No. [________]
            Plaintiff,                 )
   vs.                                 )  [DEFENDANT'S] MOTION FOR
[DEFENDANT NAME],                      )  [SPECIFIC RELIEF]
            Defendant.                 )

   COMES NOW [Movant], [Plaintiff / Defendant], appearing
[through counsel / pro se], and pursuant to I.R.C.P. 7(b) and
[specific rule, e.g., I.R.C.P. 56], moves the Court for [specific
relief].

   The grounds for this Motion, stated with particularity per
I.R.C.P. 7(b)(1), are: [concise grounds]. This Motion is supported
by the Memorandum that follows, [the Affidavit/Declaration of
[name], filed herewith,] and the records and files in this action.
```

## The Supporting Memorandum

```
       [Caption replicated; see id-statewide-format]

       MEMORANDUM IN SUPPORT OF [MOTION TITLE]

I.  INTRODUCTION
1.  [One or two paragraphs: the case, the relief sought, and the
    reasons in headline form.]

II. STATEMENT OF FACTS
2.  [Each material fact in a numbered paragraph, with a record
    citation — e.g., ([Name] Aff. ¶ 3), (Compl. ¶ 5), (Ex. A).
    Sworn facts belong in an Affidavit/Declaration; see
    id-draft-declaration.]

III. ARGUMENT
    A.  [Headline of first argument.]
3.  [Lead with the rule; state the controlling standard; apply it
    to the facts; cite controlling Idaho authority — Idaho Reports
    + Pacific Reporter, e.g., Smith v. Jones, ___ Idaho ___, ___
    P.3d ___ (YEAR) or (Ct. App. YEAR).]
    B.  [Headline of second argument.]

IV. CONCLUSION
4.  For the foregoing reasons, [Movant] respectfully requests that
    the Court [grant the relief sought] and sign the accompanying
    proposed Order.

                                  ____________________________
                                  [Name], [ISB No. ____ if attorney;
                                  self-represented filers omit the
                                  ISB number — I.R.C.P. 11]
                                  [Address, telephone, email]
                                  Attorney for / Pro se [party]
```

The **Notice of Hearing** is a separate document (`id-draft-note`); the
**proposed Order** is a separate document (`id-draft-order`).

## Certificate of Service (I.R.C.P. 5)

Papers served after the complaint require a certificate of service.

```
                  CERTIFICATE OF SERVICE

   I certify that on [date] I served a true and correct copy of
the foregoing on the following, by [iCourt e-service / U.S. mail,
postage prepaid / hand delivery]:

   [Name and address of each party/attorney served]

                                  ____________________________
                                  [Name]
```

See `id-deadlines` for the I.R.C.P. 2.2 day count and the mail add-on.
