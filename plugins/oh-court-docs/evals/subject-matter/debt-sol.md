# oh-consumer-debt — Ohio SOL on debt

## Prompt

What's the statute of limitations on a credit-card debt
in Ohio?

## Expected triggers

- `oh-consumer-debt`
- `oh-law-references`

## Acceptance criteria

- [ ] **6 years** on written contracts under **R.C.
      2305.06**
- [ ] Notes the 2021 S.B. 13 reduction from 8 → 6 years
- [ ] Notes prior 2012 S.B. 224 reduction from 15 → 8
      years
- [ ] Notes 4-year SOL on UCC Article 3 negotiable-
      instruments suits under **R.C. 1303.16**
- [ ] Notes the clock typically runs from date of default
      / last payment (subject to acknowledgment-revival
      rules under R.C. 2305.08)

## Common failure modes

- Stating 15 years (pre-2012 law)
- Stating 8 years (pre-2021 law)
- Confusing oral / written contract SOLs
- Missing acknowledgment-revival caveat
