# oh-draft-note — Notice of Hearing

## Prompt

I need to set my motion for hearing in Franklin County
Common Pleas. The motion was filed two weeks ago. How do I
draft a Notice of Hearing?

## Expected triggers

- `oh-draft-note`
- `oh-frank`
- `oh-schedule-hearing`
- `oh-statewide-format`

## Acceptance criteria

### Structure

- [ ] Civ. R. 10(A) caption: `IN THE COURT OF COMMON
      PLEAS OF FRANKLIN COUNTY, OHIO`
- [ ] Title: `NOTICE OF HEARING` (Ohio uses "Notice of
      Hearing", NOT "Notice of Motion" — that's California
      practice)
- [ ] Identifies the motion being set
- [ ] Date / time / courtroom / department
- [ ] Civ. R. 5(B) certificate of service

### Process notes

- [ ] Notes that in Ohio, hearings are typically set by
      the **court / magistrate's staff**, not by the
      movant — movant submits a notice AFTER coordinating
      a date with chambers (oral or via local-rule
      scheduling protocol)
- [ ] References Franklin County local rules for chambers
      contact / scheduling form

## Common failure modes

- Titling it "Notice of Motion" (California / federal
  style)
- Self-selecting a hearing date without confirming with
  chambers (Ohio courts assign dates)
- Wrong court name in caption
- Missing service certificate
