# oh-hearings — Magistrate hearing prep

## Prompt

I have a magistrate hearing scheduled in 2 weeks in my
Ohio Common Pleas case. What should I prepare, and what
happens after the magistrate decides?

## Expected triggers

- `oh-hearings`
- `oh-post-judgment`

## Acceptance criteria

### Prep

- [ ] References Civ. R. 53 magistrate framework
- [ ] References Cisco Webex or other remote-appearance
      conventions (statewide and county-specific)
- [ ] Notes courtesy-copy / chambers-copy conventions

### Post-decision

- [ ] **14-day written-objection clock under Civ. R.
      53(D)(3)(b)** — this is the critical Ohio quirk
- [ ] Objections must be in writing, filed with the trial
      judge, and specifically identify each finding /
      conclusion challenged
- [ ] **Failure to object generally waives the right to
      appellate review of all but plain error**

## Common failure modes

- Treating the magistrate's decision as immediately final
- Missing the 14-day objection clock
- Filing an oral objection only
- Not specifically identifying the challenged findings
