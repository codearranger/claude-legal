# oh-law-references — Evidence rules lookup

## Prompt

I'm preparing a foundation objection at an Ohio Common
Pleas trial. The plaintiff's "custodian of records"
affidavit on a credit-card statement seems weak. What
Ohio rule governs business-records foundation?

## Expected triggers

- `oh-law-references`
- `oh-fact-check`

## Acceptance criteria

- [ ] Cites **Evid. R. 803(6)** (business-records hearsay
      exception) — Ohio analog of FRE 803(6)
- [ ] Cites **Evid. R. 901** (authentication) and notes
      the elements the custodian must establish
- [ ] References *LVNV Funding, LLC v. Henderson*,
      2018-Ohio-3535 or comparable Ohio appellate cite on
      debt-buyer custodian affidavits
- [ ] Notes the alternative of Evid. R. 902(11) self-
      authentication

## Common failure modes

- Citing FRE 803(6) only (not Ohio)
- Missing the business-records foundational elements
- No Ohio case law cited
