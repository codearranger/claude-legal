# Requests for Admission bank — debt-buyer (Ark. R. Civ. P. 36)

> **NOT LEGAL ADVICE.** A drafting aid, not advice. Tailor every
> request to your facts; verify the current Rule 36 mechanics and the
> response/deadline rules (keep day counts in `ar-deadlines`).
> Available as of right in **Circuit Court**.

**Rule:** Ark. R. Civ. P. 36 — requests for admission. A matter is
**deemed admitted** unless the responding party serves a written
answer or objection within the Rule-36 period (verify the current day
count). This makes RFAs a powerful tool: a debt buyer that **cannot**
prove a link must either admit the gap or deny — and a false denial
that the plaintiff cannot back up at trial can support cost-shifting
under Rule 37(c).

These admissions are drafted so that a plaintiff lacking the
chain-of-title and account documentation cannot truthfully **deny**
them.

## Definitions

- **"THE ACCOUNT"** — the account identified in the complaint, number
  ending [____].
- **"THE ORIGINAL CREDITOR"** — [____].
- **"YOU" / "PLAINTIFF"** — the plaintiff and its agents and
  predecessors.

## Ownership / chain-of-title admissions

1. Admit YOU are **not** THE ORIGINAL CREDITOR of THE ACCOUNT.
2. Admit YOU **purchased** THE ACCOUNT rather than originated it.
3. Admit YOU do **not** possess a **Bill of Sale** that specifically
   identifies THE ACCOUNT by account number.
4. Admit YOU do **not** possess an **Assignment** that specifically
   identifies THE ACCOUNT by account number.
5. Admit there is **at least one intermediate purchaser** between THE
   ORIGINAL CREDITOR and YOU for which YOU lack a bill of sale tied to
   THE ACCOUNT.
6. Admit YOU cannot produce the **account-level data file** showing
   THE ACCOUNT was within the pool sold to YOU.

## Account / amount admissions

7. Admit YOU do **not** possess the **signed** cardholder/credit
   agreement bearing the defendant's signature for THE ACCOUNT.
8. Admit YOU do **not** possess **all periodic statements** for THE
   ACCOUNT.
9. Admit YOU cannot identify the **date of the last payment** on THE
   ACCOUNT from YOUR own records.
10. Admit the amount claimed includes **interest and/or fees** added
    after charge-off.

## SOL admissions

11. Admit the **last payment** on THE ACCOUNT was made on or before
    [date].
12. Admit **more than [3 / 5] years** elapsed between the date of last
    activity and the date this suit was filed.

## License admissions

13. Admit YOU do **not** hold a current **Arkansas collection-agency
    license** from the Arkansas State Board of Collection Agencies.
    *(Use only after confirming the plaintiff is a covered entity and
    checking the Board roster — see `ar-collection-agency-law.md`.)*

## Foundation admissions

14. Admit YOUR affiant has **no personal knowledge** of THE ORIGINAL
    CREDITOR's record-keeping practices.
15. Admit YOU intend to rely on an Ark. R. Evid. **902(11)**
    certification to authenticate THE ACCOUNT records.
