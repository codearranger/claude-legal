# Interrogatory bank — debt-buyer (Ark. R. Civ. P. 33)

> **NOT LEGAL ADVICE.** A drafting aid, not advice. Tailor every
> interrogatory to your facts; verify the current Rule 33 mechanics
> and response timing (keep day counts in `ar-deadlines`). Available
> as of right in **Circuit Court**.

**Rule:** Ark. R. Civ. P. 33 — interrogatories to parties. Arkansas
**does** permit written interrogatories. **Note the presumptive
numeric cap:** Rule 33 limits the number of interrogatories (including
discrete subparts) a party may serve without leave of court or
stipulation — **verify the current cap** (commonly noted as **30**)
before serving, and count subparts. If you need more, seek leave or a
stipulation.

These interrogatories force the plaintiff to **identify** the chain of
title, the custodian/affiant's basis of knowledge, the account-level
data, and its license status — the matters it must prove and often
cannot.

## Definitions

- **"THE ACCOUNT"** — the account in the complaint, number ending
  [____].
- **"THE ORIGINAL CREDITOR"** — [____].
- **"IDENTIFY"** (a document) — state its date, author, custodian, and
  contents; (a person) — state name, role, employer, and contact.

## Chain-of-title interrogatories

1. **IDENTIFY** THE ORIGINAL CREDITOR of THE ACCOUNT and the date it
   was opened.
2. State, **in order**, every entity that owned THE ACCOUNT from THE
   ORIGINAL CREDITOR to YOU, with the **date** of each transfer.
3. For each transfer, **IDENTIFY** the Bill of Sale and Assignment and
   state whether it specifically names THE ACCOUNT by account number.
4. Describe the **account-level data** YOU received with THE ACCOUNT
   and IDENTIFY the file/record in which it is kept.
5. State the **price or formula** by which THE ACCOUNT was sold to YOU
   (or state that YOU contend it is irrelevant and why).

## Account / amount interrogatories

6. State the **date and amount of the last payment** on THE ACCOUNT
   and the source of that information.
7. State the **charge-off date** and charge-off balance.
8. **Itemize** the amount claimed: principal, interest, and each fee,
   with the date and authority for each.

## SOL interrogatories

9. State the **date YOU contend the limitations period began** to run
   and the period YOU contend applies, with the basis.
10. State whether YOU contend THE ACCOUNT is a **written contract** or
    an **open account**, and the basis.

## Foundation / affiant interrogatories

11. **IDENTIFY** each affiant or witness YOU will offer to authenticate
    THE ACCOUNT records, and state the **basis of that person's
    knowledge** of THE ORIGINAL CREDITOR's record-keeping.
12. State whether YOU will rely on an Ark. R. Evid. **902(11)**
    certification, and IDENTIFY the certifying person.

## License interrogatories

13. State YOUR **Arkansas collection-agency license number** and
    status, or state the basis for any claim that YOU are **exempt**
    from licensure. *(Use after confirming covered-entity status — see
    `ar-collection-agency-law.md`.)*
14. **IDENTIFY** any servicer/agent collecting THE ACCOUNT and state
    its Arkansas license status.
