# Online sources — Arkansas consumer-debt defense

> **NOT LEGAL ADVICE.** URLs change; verify against the live site.
> Always confirm statutory text against the **current** Ark. Code
> Ann., not a cached copy.

## Arkansas primary law

- **Arkansas Code (Ark. Code Ann.)** — official: the General Assembly
  at `https://arkleg.state.ar.us/` (Arkansas Code search). Free
  structured mirror: Justia, `https://law.justia.com/codes/arkansas/`.
  - Statute of limitations: § 16-56-111 (written contract, 5 yrs);
    § 16-56-105 (oral/open account, 3 yrs); § 16-56-114 / § 16-65-501
    (judgments / revival).
  - ADTPA: § 4-88-101 et seq. (Title 4, ch. 88).
  - Collection agencies: § 17-24-101 et seq. (Title 17, ch. 24).
  - UCC Article 9: § 4-9-101 et seq.
  - Attorney's fees: § 16-22-308.
- **Arkansas Judiciary** — `https://www.arcourts.gov/` — court rules
  (Arkansas Rules of Civil Procedure, Arkansas Rules of Evidence,
  Arkansas District Court Rules, Administrative Orders), forms, and
  the rules of the Supreme Court and Court of Appeals (incl. Rule
  5-2 citation).
- **Court rules free mirror** — `https://www.courtrules.net/` (a
  structured free aggregator of state court rules).

## Arkansas regulators

- **Arkansas State Board of Collection Agencies** — the licensing /
  enforcement body for collection agencies; check the **licensee
  roster** to verify a plaintiff's or servicer's license status
  before asserting an unlicensed-collector defense. (Locate the
  current Board page via `https://portal.arkansas.gov/` /
  `https://www.arkansas.gov/` agency directory; confirm the live URL.)
- **Arkansas Attorney General — Consumer Protection Division** —
  `https://arkansasag.gov/` — ADTPA enforcement and the consumer
  complaint intake (the path for conduct that cannot satisfy the
  post-Act-986 private elements; see `ar-adtpa.md`).
