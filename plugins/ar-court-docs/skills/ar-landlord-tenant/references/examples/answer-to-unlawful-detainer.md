**Authoring notes (delete before any real use):**

- District Court is the civil unlawful-detainer / eviction forum;
  appeals run **de novo to Circuit Court** (confirm the appeal window
  in `ar-deadlines`). The caption shape is the District Court form —
  see `ar-statewide-format` and `ar-district-courts`.
- Do **not** confuse the civil unlawful-detainer track (Ark. Code Ann.
  § 18-60-301 et seq.) with the criminal failure-to-vacate statute
  (Ark. Code Ann. § 18-16-101); they are different proceedings — see
  `ar-landlord-tenant`.
- The **habitability** defense applies only to leases made after the
  Act 1052 of 2021 effective date and carries a **limited** remedy
  (written notice → statutory cure period → terminate and recover
  deposit; no rent-withholding / repair-and-deduct). Plead it to your
  actual dates and conditions.
- The **security-deposit** statute has a small-landlord exemption;
  confirm the landlord is covered before relying on it.
- Keep all day counts and the exact notice period in the references
  corpus, not hard-coded here.
