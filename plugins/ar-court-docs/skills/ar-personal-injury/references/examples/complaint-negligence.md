**Authoring notes (delete before any real use):**

- The initiating Complaint is served with a **summons under Ark. R.
  Civ. P. 4**, not by a Rule 5 certificate of service; confirm the
  Rule 4 service period and method in `ar-deadlines` / `ar-law-references`.
- Arkansas is a **fact-pleading** jurisdiction (Ark. R. Civ. P. 8(a));
  plead the concrete facts of the collision, not bare conclusions, to
  survive a Rule 12(b)(6) motion — see `ar-first-30-days`.
- Plead the **negligence elements** (duty / breach / causation /
  damages) expressly and tie each to the facts.
- The **modified comparative-fault** framework (§ 16-64-122) is noted
  in the body; keep the exact bar threshold and any several-liability
  allocation in the references corpus rather than hard-coding it — see
  `ar-personal-injury`.
- Arkansas currently has **no statutory cap** on personal-injury
  damages (Ark. Const. art. 5, § 32); do not plead a cap.
- Confirm the **SOL** for the claim before filing — keep the period in
  `ar-statutes-of-limitations.md`.
