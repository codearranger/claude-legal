# Arkansas Citation Format

> **NOT LEGAL ADVICE.** Citation-form reference for drafting. The
> governing authority is **Ark. Sup. Ct. R. 5-2**; read it for the
> controlling form.

Arkansas has **no formal state style manual**. Citation practice is
Bluebook-based, **modified by Ark. Sup. Ct. R. 5-2**, which establishes
the **medium-neutral / public-domain citation** for Arkansas appellate
opinions issued on or after **July 1, 2009**.

## Cases

### Medium-neutral citation (opinions on/after July 1, 2009)

Under **Ark. Sup. Ct. R. 5-2**, opinions issued on or after July 1,
2009 carry a **public-domain citation** consisting of the year, the
court designation, and a sequential opinion number — with paragraph
numbers for pinpoint cites:

- **Arkansas Supreme Court**: `Johnson v. Rockwell Automation, Inc., 2009 Ark. 241`
- **Arkansas Court of Appeals**: `Smith v. Jones, 2015 Ark. App. 200`
- **Pinpoint** (paragraph): `Bayer CropScience LP v. Schafer, 2011 Ark. 518, at ¶ 12`
- A **parallel** South Western Reporter cite is commonly added but the
  medium-neutral cite is the **required** form:
  `Bd. of Trustees of Univ. of Ark. v. Andrews, 2018 Ark. 12, 535 S.W.3d 616`.

### Pre-July-1-2009 opinions

Cite to the **Arkansas Reports** (`Ark.`) / **Arkansas Appellate
Reports** (`Ark. App.`) plus the **South Western Reporter** (`S.W.`,
`S.W.2d`, `S.W.3d`):

- `Sterling Drug, Inc. v. Oxford, 294 Ark. 239, 743 S.W.2d 380 (1988)`
- `Wallace v. Broyles, 331 Ark. 58, 961 S.W.2d 712 (1998)`

> The **Arkansas Reports** and **Arkansas Appellate Reports** were
> **discontinued** as bound print reporters in connection with the move
> to medium-neutral citation; modern Arkansas opinions are cited by the
> `YYYY Ark. NNN` / `YYYY Ark. App. NNN` form. [VERIFY edge cases near
> the 2009 transition against Rule 5-2.]

## Statutes

- **Arkansas Code Annotated**: `Ark. Code Ann. § 16-56-111`.
- Subsections: `Ark. Code Ann. § 4-88-113(f)`.
- Ranges / "et seq.": `Ark. Code Ann. § 17-24-101 et seq.`

## Rules

- **Civil**: `Ark. R. Civ. P. 12`; with subpart `Ark. R. Civ. P. 12(b)(6)`.
- **Evidence**: `Ark. R. Evid. 803(6)`; `Ark. R. Evid. 902(11)`.
- **Supreme Court rules**: `Ark. Sup. Ct. R. 5-2`; appellate-brief
  format `Ark. Sup. Ct. R. 4-2`.
- **District Court Rules** (formerly Inferior Court Rules):
  `Ark. Dist. Ct. R. [N]` [VERIFY the current abbreviation].
- **Administrative Orders**: `Ark. Sup. Ct. Admin. Order No. 19`
  (and No. 10, No. 21).

## Constitutional provisions

- `Ark. Const. art. 5, § 32` (no damages caps for death / personal
  injury); `Ark. Const. art. 5, § 20` (State immunity); `Ark. Const.
  amend. 80` (court unification); `Ark. Const. amend. 34`
  (right-to-work).

## Federal authority (when cited alongside Arkansas law)

- **U.S. Code**: `15 U.S.C. § 1692g(b)`.
- **C.F.R.**: `12 C.F.R. § 1006.34` (Reg F).
- **Cases**: standard Bluebook — `Reporter` + `(Court Year)`.

## Quick checklist

- Post-7/1/2009 Arkansas opinion → **medium-neutral** `YYYY Ark. NNN` /
  `YYYY Ark. App. NNN` (add the `S.W.3d` parallel if available).
- Pre-7/1/2009 → `Ark.` / `Ark. App.` + `S.W.(2d/3d)`.
- Always pinpoint to the **paragraph** (¶) in a medium-neutral cite,
  not a page.
- Confirm whether you must flag an opinion's published status — in
  Arkansas the appellate courts designate certain Court of Appeals
  dispositions; verify citability of any unpublished memorandum opinion.
