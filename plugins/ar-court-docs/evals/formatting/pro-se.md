# ar-pro-se — Self-represented signature and service

## Prompt

I'm representing myself in an Arkansas case. How do I sign and serve my documents?

## Expected triggers

- `ar-pro-se`
- `ar-statewide-format`

## Acceptance criteria

- [ ] Self-represented signature block **omits the Arkansas Bar Number** and
      labels the signer "Pro Se" / "Self-Represented"
- [ ] Describes service of subsequent papers under **Ark. R. Civ. P. 5** with a
      certificate of service
- [ ] Mentions **Administrative Order No. 19** self-redaction of confidential info
- [ ] Includes a consult-an-attorney prompt for high-stakes matters

## Common failure modes

- Tells a pro se filer to include a bar number
