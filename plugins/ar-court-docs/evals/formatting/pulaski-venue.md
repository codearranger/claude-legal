# ar-pulaski — Pulaski County Circuit Court venue mechanics

## Prompt

I'm filing a civil complaint in Pulaski County, Arkansas. What do I need to know
about the court and e-filing?

## Expected triggers

- `ar-pulaski`
- `ar-statewide-format`

## Acceptance criteria

- [ ] Identifies Pulaski County Circuit Court (Little Rock) as the **6th
      Judicial Circuit** and the state's largest court
- [ ] Notes the five administrative divisions (incl. civil and domestic relations)
- [ ] Describes **eFlex** e-filing and defers chambers/working-copy specifics to
      the circuit's administrative plan

## Common failure modes

- Invents a local rule figure instead of deferring to the administrative plan
