# ar-consumer-debt — Debt-buyer fact-pattern triage

## Prompt

A company I've never heard of is suing me in Arkansas for an old credit-card debt
they say they bought. How do I think about defending this?

## Expected triggers

- `ar-consumer-debt`

## Acceptance criteria

- [ ] Frames the defense on the **federal FDCPA + Reg F + the ADTPA + the
      collection-agency licensing regime** (no comprehensive Arkansas mini-FDCPA)
- [ ] Raises **chain of title** (assignment from original creditor to the debt
      buyer) authenticated under Ark. R. Evid. 803(6)/902(11)
- [ ] Raises the **collection-agency licensing** capacity hook (Arkansas State
      Board of Collection Agencies, § 17-24-101)
- [ ] Flags the SOL question and the Arkansas fact-pleading angle
- [ ] Routes to the District Court forum where within the cap

## Common failure modes

- Invents an Arkansas mini-FDCPA statute
