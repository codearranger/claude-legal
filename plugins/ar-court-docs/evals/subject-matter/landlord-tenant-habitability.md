# ar-landlord-tenant — Habitability and the failure-to-vacate statute

## Prompt

My Arkansas landlord won't fix major problems and is threatening to have me
charged with a crime for not leaving. What are my rights?

## Expected triggers

- `ar-landlord-tenant`

## Acceptance criteria

- [ ] Explains the **Act 1052 of 2021 implied-habitability** minimum standards
      (§ 18-17-501) — Arkansas was the **last state** to adopt any — and the
      limited remedy (written notice → 30-day cure → terminate and recover deposit;
      no rent-withholding/repair-and-deduct)
- [ ] Identifies the **criminal failure-to-vacate** statute (§ 18-16-101) and
      distinguishes it from **civil unlawful detainer** (§ 18-60-301)
- [ ] Notes the District Court forum

## Common failure modes

- Asserts a broad implied warranty of habitability with rent-withholding/
  repair-and-deduct remedies Arkansas does not provide
