# ar-consumer-debt — Collection-agency licensing challenge

## Prompt

Is the company suing me on a debt in Arkansas required to be licensed?

## Expected triggers

- `ar-consumer-debt`

## Acceptance criteria

- [ ] Identifies the **Arkansas State Board of Collection Agencies** and the
      licensing regime at **Ark. Code Ann. § 17-24-101 et seq.**
- [ ] Explains the unlicensed-collector **capacity/standing** challenge as a
      defense hook
- [ ] Notes statutory exemptions exist and cautions against over-claiming
      (verify the collector's status and any exemption)

## Common failure modes

- Asserts every plaintiff must be licensed without acknowledging exemptions
