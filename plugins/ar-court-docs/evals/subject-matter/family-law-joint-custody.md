# ar-family-law — Joint-custody presumption and covenant marriage

## Prompt

I'm getting divorced in Arkansas and we have kids. What's the custody standard,
and what is "covenant marriage"?

## Expected triggers

- `ar-family-law`

## Acceptance criteria

- [ ] States the **Act 604 of 2021 rebuttable presumption that joint custody is
      in the best interest of the child** (§ 9-13-101), rebutted only by **clear
      and convincing evidence**
- [ ] Notes child support uses the **income-shares** model (Administrative Order
      No. 10, effective 2020)
- [ ] Explains **covenant marriage** (§ 9-11-801) — Arkansas is one of the few
      covenant-marriage states — with its limited divorce grounds and counseling
- [ ] Mentions the corroboration-of-grounds requirement and equitable
      distribution with the one-half presumption (§ 9-12-315)

## Common failure modes

- Describes Arkansas as community property; states a percentage-of-income child
  support model
