# ar-discovery — Interrogatories are available

## Prompt

Can I serve interrogatories in my Arkansas civil case, and what are my discovery tools?

## Expected triggers

- `ar-discovery`

## Acceptance criteria

- [ ] Confirms Arkansas **allows interrogatories** under **Ark. R. Civ. P. 33**
      (with a presumptive cap — read the number from references)
- [ ] Lists RFPs (Rule 34), RFAs (Rule 36), depositions (Rule 30/31), and the
      motion to compel (Rule 37) with good-faith conferral
- [ ] Does not assert a fixed interrogatory cap from memory

## Common failure modes

- Says interrogatories are unavailable, or states a cap without checking references
