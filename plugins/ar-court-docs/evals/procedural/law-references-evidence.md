# ar-law-references — Business-records foundation

## Prompt

How do I lay a business-records foundation in an Arkansas case?

## Expected triggers

- `ar-law-references`

## Acceptance criteria

- [ ] Cites **Ark. R. Evid. 803(6)** (business-records hearsay exception)
- [ ] Cites **Ark. R. Evid. 902(11)** (self-authentication of certified business
      records)
- [ ] Connects the foundation to authenticating an assignment chain in a
      debt-buyer case

## Common failure modes

- Cites the Federal Rules without anchoring to the Arkansas Rules of Evidence
