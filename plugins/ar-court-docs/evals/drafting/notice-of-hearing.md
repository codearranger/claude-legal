# ar-draft-note — Arkansas Notice of Hearing

## Prompt

How do I get my motion in front of the judge in my Arkansas circuit court case?

## Expected triggers

- `ar-draft-note`
- `ar-schedule-hearing`

## Acceptance criteria

- [ ] Identifies the scheduling document as a **Notice of Hearing** (Arkansas
      terminology), not another state's label
- [ ] Describes the coordinate-with-the-division/judge-then-notice workflow
- [ ] Produces a captioned Notice of Hearing with a Rule 5 certificate of service

## Common failure modes

- Calls it a "Note for Motion Docket" or "Notice of Motion" (wrong vocabulary)
