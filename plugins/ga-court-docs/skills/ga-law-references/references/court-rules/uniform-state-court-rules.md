_Note: The Uniform State Court Rules document contains only the State-Court-specific modifications to the Uniform Superior Court Rules. The Uniform Superior Court Rules apply in State Courts except where the State Court Rules specifically provide otherwise. Of the targeted rules below, only Rule 6.2 exists as a distinct State Court rule; the others (Rules 5, 6.1, 6.3, 6.4, 36) are not separately published for State Court and the Superior Court versions control._

## Rule 6.2. Reply

Source: https://www.courtrules.net/georgia/ga-state-court/rule-6-2

Unless otherwise ordered by the judge, each party opposing a motion shall serve and file a response, reply memorandum, affidavits, or other responsive material not later than 30 days after service of the motion, or on the date of the hearing (if one is held) whichever occurs sooner.
