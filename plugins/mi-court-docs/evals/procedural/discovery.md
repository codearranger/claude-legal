# mi-discovery — Motion to compel under MCR 2.313 after MCR 2.302 disclosures

## Prompt

I'm suing in Michigan and the other side blew off my discovery
requests and never served the initial disclosures they were
supposed to. I tried emailing their lawyer twice and got
nothing. I want to file a motion to make them respond. How do I
do that in Michigan?

## Expected triggers

- `mi-discovery`
- `mi-draft-motion`
- `mi-statewide-format`

## Acceptance criteria

### The disclosure / discovery framework

- [ ] Identifies the Michigan discovery framework in the
      **MCR 2.300-series** and notes that **MCR 2.302**
      governs general provisions and the **required initial
      disclosures** Michigan adopted — cite MCR 2.302 and read
      the current disclosure obligations/timing from the
      references corpus rather than asserting them from memory
- [ ] Identifies the specific discovery device at issue
      (interrogatories MCR 2.309, production MCR 2.310, etc.) by
      rule number

### Meet-and-confer prerequisite

- [ ] Flags the **good-faith effort to obtain the
      discovery/disclosure without court action** required before
      a motion to compel, and that the motion must include a
      statement certifying that effort — cite the controlling
      rule (MCR 2.313 / MCR 2.302) and read the current
      certification requirement from the corpus

### The motion to compel (MCR 2.313)

- [ ] Identifies **MCR 2.313** as the rule governing motions to
      compel and sanctions for failure to disclose / cooperate in
      discovery
- [ ] Notes the **expense/sanction** provisions under MCR 2.313
      (the court may order the non-complying party to pay
      reasonable expenses, including attorney fees) and the range
      of available sanctions — read current specifics from the
      corpus rather than asserting them
- [ ] Drafts the motion per `mi-draft-motion` formatting with the
      MCR 2.313 caption/title

## Common failure modes

- Skips the meet-and-confer / certification prerequisite
- Cites FRCP 37 instead of MCR 2.313
- Ignores the MCR 2.302 required-disclosures angle
- Asserts the sanction menu from memory rather than the corpus
