# or-draft-motion — Motion to Compel scaffold

## Prompt

Draft a motion to compel for my Oregon case. Plaintiff
(Velocity Investments, LLC) served bogus objections to my
RFPs targeting the chain of title. The case is in Multnomah
Co Circuit Court, 25CV01234. I'm pro se.

## Expected triggers

- `or-draft-motion`
- `or-statewide-format`
- `or-multcc`
- `or-discovery`
- `or-pro-se`

## Acceptance criteria

### Caption

- [ ] Correct Multnomah court header
- [ ] Case number `25CV01234`
- [ ] Title in ALL CAPS: "DEFENDANT'S MOTION TO COMPEL
      PRODUCTION OF DOCUMENTS UNDER ORCP 46 A"
- [ ] Oral argument designation included

### Motion section

- [ ] Cites ORCP 46 A on the first line
- [ ] Lists specific relief sought (production deadline,
      overruling objections, fees under ORCP 46 A(4)(a))

### Memorandum sections

- [ ] I. Relief Requested
- [ ] II. Statement of Facts (with Decl. ¶ N citations)
- [ ] III. Statement of Issues (yes/no questions)
- [ ] IV. Evidence Relied Upon
- [ ] V. Authorities (ORCP 36 B(1), ORCP 43, ORCP 46 A,
      ORCP 46 A(4)(a), Multnomah SLR 5.045)
- [ ] VI. Argument (organized by issue with bold lead-ins)
- [ ] VII. Conclusion

### Meet-and-confer certification

- [ ] Present at the end of the motion
- [ ] References Multnomah SLR 5.045 and ORCP 46 A
- [ ] Recites the specific dates of M&C correspondence

### Pro se conventions

- [ ] "Defendant, pro se" in signature block
- [ ] No OSB#

## Common failure modes

- Forgetting the Multnomah SLR 5.045 certification
- Citing FRCP 26 or 37 instead of ORCP
- Citing Washington CR 37 instead of ORCP 46
- Using "vs." instead of "v."
- Mis-formatting ORCP subsection (`ORCP 46(A)` instead of
  `ORCP 46 A`)
