# or-draft-note — Notice of Hearing

## Prompt

The Multnomah JA confirmed my hearing for July 15 at 10 AM
on WebEx in Judge Smith's department. I'm in case 25CV01234.
I need to draft a Notice of Hearing for my Motion to Compel.
I'm pro se.

## Expected triggers

- `or-draft-note`
- `or-statewide-format`
- `or-multcc`

## Acceptance criteria

### Title

- [ ] "NOTICE OF HEARING ON [MOTION TITLE]" — Oregon
      terminology (NOT "Note for Motion Docket" which is
      Washington)

### Caption

- [ ] Correct Multnomah court header
- [ ] Case number `25CV01234`
- [ ] Hearing details on right side: date, time, mode,
      courtroom/WebEx, judge

### Body

- [ ] "TO: [Plaintiff and counsel]" line
- [ ] "PLEASE TAKE NOTICE" formal language
- [ ] Identifies the motion by title
- [ ] States hearing date, time, place, mode
- [ ] Includes WebEx connection info (if applicable)
- [ ] Estimated argument time
- [ ] References that the hearing was reserved with the JA

### Pro se signature

- [ ] "Defendant, pro se"
- [ ] No OSB#
- [ ] Contact info

### Multnomah-specific

- [ ] References Multnomah SLR 5.015 (3 business days to
      file after JA confirms)

## Common failure modes

- Using "Note for Motion Docket" (Washington terminology)
- Suggesting filing simultaneously with the motion (that's
  Washington Co SLR 5.045, not Multnomah)
- Missing the WebEx connection details
- Wrong courtroom or judge identification
- Filing the Notice before JA confirmation
