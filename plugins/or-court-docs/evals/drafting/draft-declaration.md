# or-draft-declaration — Declaration scaffold

## Prompt

I need to write a declaration to support my motion to
compel in Oregon. I'm Jane Doe, pro se, in Multnomah case
25CV01234.

## Expected triggers

- `or-draft-declaration`
- `or-statewide-format`
- `or-multcc`
- `or-pro-se`

## Acceptance criteria

### Structure

- [ ] Caption identifying "DECLARATION OF JANE DOE IN
      SUPPORT OF [MOTION]"
- [ ] Salutation: "I, Jane Doe, declare under penalty of
      perjury under the laws of the State of Oregon..."
- [ ] Numbered paragraphs
- [ ] Verification clause at end: "I declare under penalty
      of perjury..." (ORCP 1 E)
- [ ] "DATED this ____ day of __________, 20__"
- [ ] "Executed at [City], Oregon"
- [ ] Signature block with "Defendant, pro se"
- [ ] Exhibit list (if exhibits)
- [ ] Cover pages per exhibit

### Content guidance

- [ ] First paragraph: identity and purpose
- [ ] Subsequent paragraphs: one fact each, with
      authentication of exhibits
- [ ] Personal-knowledge basis stated
- [ ] No argument mixed with facts

### Oregon conventions

- [ ] **Numbered** exhibits (1, 2, 3), NOT lettered
- [ ] "Executed at [city], Oregon" with state name
- [ ] ORCP 1 E form (not the WA RCW 9A.72.085 form)

## Common failure modes

- Using "I, [Name], do hereby state under oath..." (notarized
  affidavit form; not needed in Oregon under ORCP 1 E)
- Wrong jurisdiction in salutation ("State of Washington")
- Lettered exhibits
- Argument in the declaration (belongs in the motion)
- Missing the verification clause at the end
