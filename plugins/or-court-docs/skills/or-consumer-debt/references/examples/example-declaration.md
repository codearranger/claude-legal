## Key features illustrated

1. **ORCP 1 E perjury clause** at the top and bottom
2. **Numbered paragraphs** with one fact per paragraph
3. **Bold lead-ins** (Identity, Service, etc.) to help the
   reader navigate
4. **Personal knowledge** — every fact within the declarant's
   personal observation
5. **No argument** — pure facts. Argument lives in the
   motion.
6. **Exhibit authentication** — each exhibit is referenced
   with "A true and correct copy is attached as Exhibit N"
7. **Numbered exhibits** (1, 2, 3, 4) — Oregon convention
8. **"Executed at Portland, Oregon"** — required per ORCP 1 E
9. **Pro se signature block**
10. **Exhibit List** with brief description of each
11. **Exhibit cover pages** with title and italic caption

## What this declaration does NOT do

- Make legal arguments (those are in the motion)
- Make characterizations ("Plaintiff is acting in bad faith")
- Speculate about Plaintiff's motives
- Discuss the merits of the underlying claim

The declaration sticks to facts the declarant personally
observed.
