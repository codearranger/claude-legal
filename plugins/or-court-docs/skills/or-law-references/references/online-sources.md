# Online Sources — Canonical URLs for Oregon Civil Practice

This is the AI-agent-friendly catalog of authoritative URLs for
fetching current Oregon law, court rules, and case opinions.

**Per the plugin's safety policy**: WebFetch is the only approved
fetch mechanism. No curl, wget, Python requests, or other
network calls. All sources below are public-facing HTML or PDF
pages that WebFetch can read.

## Oregon court rules

### Oregon Rules of Civil Procedure (ORCP)

- **Council on Court Procedures**:
  https://counciloncourtprocedures.org/
- **OJD rules page**:
  https://www.courts.oregon.gov/rules/Pages/orcp.aspx

The Council on Court Procedures (CCP) is the primary
authoritative source — they promulgate the ORCP. The OJD page
hosts the consolidated PDF. Updates effective January 1 of
odd-numbered years.

### Uniform Trial Court Rules (UTCR)

- **OJD UTCR page**:
  https://www.courts.oregon.gov/rules/UTCR/Pages/default.aspx

Annual consolidated PDF, effective August 1.

### Oregon Evidence Code (OEC)

Codified at **ORS Ch. 40** (sections 40.010–40.585):

- https://www.oregonlegislature.gov/bills_laws/ors/ors040.html

### Oregon Rules of Appellate Procedure (ORAP)

- **OJD ORAP page**:
  https://www.courts.oregon.gov/rules/Pages/orap.aspx

### Supplemental Local Rules (SLR) by county

Pattern: `https://www.courts.oregon.gov/courts/[slug]/rules`

- Multnomah: /multnomah/rules
- Washington: /washington/rules
- Clackamas: /clackamas/rules
- Lane: /lane/rules
- Marion: /marion/rules
- Jackson: /jackson/rules
- Deschutes: /deschutes/rules
- ... and so on for all 27 judicial districts

Use the OJD "Find a Court" page to list all county slugs:
https://www.courts.oregon.gov/courts/Pages/find-a-court.aspx

## Oregon statutes

### ORS — Oregon Revised Statutes

- **Browse by chapter**:
  https://www.oregonlegislature.gov/bills_laws/Pages/ORS.aspx
- **Search**: same page, search bar
- **Direct chapter URL**:
  `https://www.oregonlegislature.gov/bills_laws/ors/ors[NNN].html`

Examples:
- ORS Ch. 12 (statutes of limitations):
  https://www.oregonlegislature.gov/bills_laws/ors/ors012.html
- ORS Ch. 20 (fees and costs):
  https://www.oregonlegislature.gov/bills_laws/ors/ors020.html
- ORS Ch. 18 (judgments):
  https://www.oregonlegislature.gov/bills_laws/ors/ors018.html
- ORS Ch. 36 (mandatory arbitration):
  https://www.oregonlegislature.gov/bills_laws/ors/ors036.html
- ORS Ch. 40 (Oregon Evidence Code):
  https://www.oregonlegislature.gov/bills_laws/ors/ors040.html
- ORS Ch. 90 (landlord-tenant):
  https://www.oregonlegislature.gov/bills_laws/ors/ors090.html
- ORS Ch. 646 (UTPA):
  https://www.oregonlegislature.gov/bills_laws/ors/ors646.html
- ORS Ch. 697 (collection agencies):
  https://www.oregonlegislature.gov/bills_laws/ors/ors697.html

### OAR — Oregon Administrative Rules

- **OAR online**:
  https://secure.sos.state.or.us/oard/displayDivisionRules.action

Useful for licensing rules (Collection Agency under OAR 441 /
DCBS) and other administrative regulations.

## Oregon case law

### Oregon Judicial Department opinions

- **OJD Publications**:
  https://www.publications.ojd.state.or.us/

Recent Supreme Court and Court of Appeals opinions, indexed by
date.

### CourtListener (recommended for cite-checking)

- **Oregon courts**:
  https://www.courtlistener.com/?type=o&order_by=score%20desc&court=or,orctapp
- **Search**: full-text search of opinions
- **Citation lookup**: paste a citation, get matching opinion

CourtListener has full-text search of Oregon Supreme Court and
Court of Appeals opinions back to the early 1900s. The
preferred source for cite-checking in this plugin.

### Google Scholar

- https://scholar.google.com/scholar?hl=en&as_sdt=0%2C38

Filter by court (Oregon Supreme Court, Oregon Court of Appeals,
9th Circuit). Often the fastest route for full-text search of a
case by name or holding.

### Caselaw Access Project (Harvard)

- https://case.law/

Useful for older Oregon decisions (pre-2010) where
CourtListener's coverage may be lighter. The CAP corpus
includes the bound Oregon Reports.

## Federal law

### U.S. Code

- **Browse**:
  https://uscode.house.gov/
- **XML (USLM)** for programmatic access:
  https://uscode.house.gov/download/download.shtml

### Code of Federal Regulations

- **eCFR (current)**:
  https://www.ecfr.gov/
- **eCFR API for versioned access**:
  https://www.ecfr.gov/api/versioner/

Most-relevant titles for consumer-debt and civil practice:
- 12 CFR (banking regulations; Reg F at part 1006)
- 15 USC § 1692 (FDCPA), § 1601 (TILA), § 1681 (FCRA)

### Federal court opinions

- **CourtListener** (same as above; covers 9th Circuit and
  District of Oregon)
- **PACER**:
  https://pacer.uscourts.gov/

PACER access requires registration and incurs per-page fees;
prefer CourtListener for public-record opinions.

## Safety policy reminder

- WebFetch only — no curl, wget, requests, or other network
  call mechanisms
- Quote verbatim from authoritative sources, do not paraphrase
- When the source has changed (effective-date update, rule
  renumbering, statute amendment), flag the change explicitly
- Never fabricate a URL — every fetch must point to a real,
  reachable resource
