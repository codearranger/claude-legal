# Washington RCW — Civil-Practice Corpus

A curated subset of the Revised Code of Washington covering the chapters a Washington civil practitioner is likely to cite: general civil procedure, evidence, enforcement of judgments, special proceedings, business regulation and consumer protection, family law, real-property and landlord-tenant, the UCC, administrative procedure, and a few targeted public-health / vulnerable-adult / public-assistance chapters that come up in adjacent matters.

One MD file per chapter; every section preserved verbatim with citation history and notes.

- Pulled: 2026-05-14
- Source: [app.leg.wa.gov/RCW](https://app.leg.wa.gov/RCW/)
- Coverage: 1,547 sections across 75 chapters

> **Directory name**: this corpus was originally scoped to debt-collection chapters and lives under `wa-rcw-debt/` for path stability with downstream skills. The scope is now considerably broader; see the table below.
