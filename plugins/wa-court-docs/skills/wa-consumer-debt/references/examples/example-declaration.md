# Example: Declaration in Support

> **SYNTHETIC EXAMPLE.** Names and facts fictional. Illustrates
> the Washington declaration format (RCW 9A.72.085) with numbered
> paragraphs, exhibit list, and verification clause.

---

```
                                  IN THE DISTRICT COURT OF THE
                                  STATE OF WASHINGTON FOR KING
                                  COUNTY, SOUTH DIVISION

                                  )
    ACME PORTFOLIO RECOVERY, LLC,  )    NO.  25CIV200000KCX
                                  )
                Plaintiff,        )    DECLARATION OF JANE Q.
                                  )    PUBLIC IN SUPPORT OF
          v.                      )    DEFENDANT'S MOTION TO
                                  )    COMPEL DISCOVERY
    JANE Q. PUBLIC,               )
                                  )
                Defendant.        )
                                  )
    ____________________________  )

                I, JANE Q. PUBLIC, DECLARE AS FOLLOWS:

    1.  I am the Defendant in this action and appear pro se. I
        am over the age of 18, competent to testify, and make
        this declaration on personal knowledge except where
        otherwise stated.

    2.  On January 15, 2026, I served upon Plaintiff's counsel,
        by U.S. Mail and email, my First Requests for Production
        and my First Interrogatories. A true and correct copy of
        the discovery requests as served is attached as
        **Exhibit A**.

    3.  On February 17, 2026, I received Plaintiff's Responses to
        the First Requests for Production and Answers to the First
        Interrogatories. A true and correct copy of Plaintiff's
        responses is attached as **Exhibit B**.

    4.  The only document Plaintiff produced in response to any
        RFP was a one-page "Certificate of Indebtedness" signed
        by one of Plaintiff's own employees. That document does
        not identify the bill-of-sale schedule, the original
        cardholder agreement, any intermediate assignments, or
        any monthly statements. A true and correct copy of the
        Certificate of Indebtedness is attached as **Exhibit E**.

    5.  On February 25, 2026, I sent Plaintiff's counsel a letter
        under CRLJ 26(f) identifying each deficient response and
        asking for supplementation within 10 business days. A
        true and correct copy of that letter is attached as
        **Exhibit C**.

    6.  On March 5, 2026, Plaintiff's counsel responded by
        letter, stating only that "Plaintiff stands on its
        objections" and declining to supplement any response. A
        true and correct copy of that response is attached as
        **Exhibit D**.

    7.  The out-of-pocket expenses I have incurred in preparing
        and bringing the accompanying Motion to Compel are:
        filing fee ($83.00), postage ($14.40), copy costs
        ($22.75), and printing costs ($11.00), for a total of
        $131.15. Receipts are attached as **Exhibit F**.

    8.  I have spent approximately twelve (12) hours preparing
        the motion, drafting the meet-and-confer correspondence,
        and assembling this declaration. I preserve the right to
        request a fee allowance under the applicable Washington
        statutes and rules.

                              EXHIBIT LIST

    Exhibit A — Defendant's First Requests for Production and
                First Interrogatories (served 1/15/2026)
    Exhibit B — Plaintiff's Responses to Defendant's First RFPs
                and First IROGs (served 2/17/2026)
    Exhibit C — Defendant's CRLJ 26(f) Meet-and-Confer Letter
                (sent 2/25/2026)
    Exhibit D — Plaintiff's Response Declining to Supplement
                (sent 3/5/2026)
    Exhibit E — Plaintiff's "Certificate of Indebtedness"
                (produced 2/17/2026)
    Exhibit F — Itemized Receipts for Out-of-Pocket Expenses

                              VERIFICATION

    I declare under penalty of perjury under the laws of the
    State of Washington that the foregoing is true and correct.

    Signed this ____ day of ____________, 20___, at Seattle,
    Washington.


                        _______________________________
                        JANE Q. PUBLIC
```
