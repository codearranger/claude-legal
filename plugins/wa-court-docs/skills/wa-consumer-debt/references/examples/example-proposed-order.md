# Example: Proposed Order on Motion to Compel

> **SYNTHETIC EXAMPLE.** Illustrates the `[PROPOSED]` bracket
> convention, findings/relief structure, and presenter block.
> Before filing post-hearing, use `wa-submit-order` to strip the
> bracket and apply bench modifications.

        THIS MATTER having come before the Court on Defendant's
    Motion to Compel Discovery and for an Award of Expenses; the
    Court having reviewed the Motion, the Declaration of Jane Q.
    Public in support, Plaintiff's response (if any), Defendant's
    reply (if any), and the records and files herein; and the
    Court being fully advised;

        IT IS HEREBY FOUND:

        A.  Defendant served First Requests for Production and
            First Interrogatories on January 15, 2026.

        B.  Plaintiff's responses of February 17, 2026, consisted
            of boilerplate objections and produced no responsive
            documents beyond a single "Certificate of
            Indebtedness."

        C.  Defendant satisfied the meet-and-confer requirement
            of CRLJ 26(f) by letter dated February 25, 2026, to
            which Plaintiff responded on March 5, 2026, declining
            to supplement.

        D.  Plaintiff's objections lack the specificity required
            under CRLJ 26(b) and CRLJ 34(b). No privilege log has
            been produced.

        E.  The requested documents are relevant to Plaintiff's
            claims and to Defendant's defenses and are
            proportional to the needs of the case.

        F.  No exception under CRLJ 37(a)(4) applies to bar an
            award of expenses to the prevailing movant.

        IT IS THEREFORE ORDERED:

        1.  Defendant's Motion to Compel is GRANTED.

        2.  Within 21 days of this Order, Plaintiff shall produce
            the following documents in response to Defendant's
            First Requests for Production:

              a.  The unredacted bill-of-sale schedule identifying
                  Defendant's account by account number (RFP 1);
              b.  Each intermediate assignment, bill of sale, or
                  purchase agreement transferring Defendant's
                  account, together with the schedule or exhibit
                  identifying Defendant's account in each
                  transaction (RFP 3);
              c.  The original cardholder agreement in force at
                  the origination of Defendant's account (RFP 5);
              d.  All monthly account statements from the
                  inception of the account to the date of charge-
                  off (RFP 7);
              e.  All documents showing the basis for the claimed
                  principal, interest, and fees (RFP 9).

        3.  Within the same 21-day period, Plaintiff shall
            provide complete, verified answers to Defendant's
            First Interrogatories, Nos. 1–5.

        4.  If Plaintiff withholds any document on a privilege
            claim, Plaintiff shall produce a privilege log
            compliant with CRLJ 26(b)(5) within 21 days.

        5.  Plaintiff shall pay Defendant's reasonable expenses of
            this motion under CRLJ 37(a)(4) in the amount of
            $[amount], as itemized in the Declaration of Jane Q.
            Public.

        6.  Defendant's fee-and-expense request beyond those
            itemized expenses is reserved. Defendant may submit a
            declaration and affidavit of additional fees within
            14 days; Plaintiff may respond within 7 days.

        DATED this ____ day of ____________, 20___.
