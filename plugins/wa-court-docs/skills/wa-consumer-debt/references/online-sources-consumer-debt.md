# Online Sources — Consumer-Debt Subject Matter

Companion to `wa-law-references/references/online-sources.md`,
which catalogs canonical URLs for Washington court rules, the
RCW, federal statutes/regulations in general, and case-law
research. **This file lists the subject-matter-specific sources
for consumer-debt practice.**

## Federal Regulations — Regulation F

Base: `https://www.ecfr.gov/current/title-12/chapter-X/subchapter-C/part-1006`

| Section | Purpose |
|---------|---------|
| 12 C.F.R. Part 1006 (Reg F) | Full Regulation F |
| § 1006.6 | Communication frequency limits |
| § 1006.26 | Time-barred debt collection |
| § 1006.34 | Validation notice requirements |
| § 1006.38 | Dispute and verification procedures |

Section URL pattern:

> `https://www.ecfr.gov/current/title-12/chapter-X/subchapter-C/part-1006/subpart-B/section-[NNNN.NN]`

**Reliability note**: eCFR is the official, real-time CFR.
"Current" URLs always show the version in force today. For
historical versions, use `past/YYYY-MM-DD` in place of `current`.

## Federal Statutes — FDCPA (15 U.S.C. § 1692 et seq.)

Primary official source:

- `https://www.govinfo.gov/app/collection/uscode/title-15` (U.S.
  Code on govinfo)

Scrape-friendly mirror (Cornell LII):

- `https://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/15/chapter-41/subchapter-V`
  — full FDCPA subchapter index

| Section | Purpose |
|---------|---------|
| § 1692a | Definitions |
| § 1692c | Communication with the consumer |
| § 1692d | Harassment or abuse |
| § 1692e | False or misleading representations |
| § 1692f | Unfair practices |
| § 1692g | Validation notices |
| § 1692k | Civil liability (fees, damages) |

Individual section URL: `https://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/15/[NNNN]`

**Reliability note**: Cornell LII is not the official U.S. Code,
but the text is accurate and regularly updated. Use it for
*reading*; cite the U.S. Code when filing (e.g., "15 U.S.C.
§ 1692e (2018)").
