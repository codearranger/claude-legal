## Federal Civil Rights Legal Clinic (FBA / Legal Gateway)

Free 30-minute sessions, **appointment only**; volunteer attorneys give limited advice and
referrals — **not representation**. Topics: discrimination (employment, housing, public
accommodations), search and seizure, excessive force, prisoner rights, free speech, voting
rights, gun rights, religious freedom. Accessible regardless of disability or English
fluency. The Clerk's Office is not affiliated and cannot book or answer for the clinic.

- **Seattle:** call (206) 267-7070 and press "1", or online at `www.kcba.org/nlc`.
  Sessions Thursdays (flyer: 12–2 pm and 6–8 pm; by phone except the first Thursday of the
  month, which is in person). Confirm current schedule when booking.
- **Tacoma:** call (253) 368-6690 (lines open M–Th 10 am–3 pm). Sessions two Thursdays a
  month (flyer: 12–2 pm; published schedules vary — confirm when booking). Phone sessions
  unless in-person is requested.
