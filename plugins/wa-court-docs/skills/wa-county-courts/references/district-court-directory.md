# Washington county-court directory (most populous counties)

Courthouse addresses, divisions, e-filing systems, and known
local-practice notes for the district courts (and superior court clerks,
where they differ) of Washington's most populous counties — **excluding
King County District Court, which is covered by the `wa-kcdc` skill.**

> Verify everything here against the court's own page and the Washington
> Courts directory (`https://www.courts.wa.gov/court_dir/`) before you put
> it in a caption or rely on it for a deadline. Addresses, division
> assignments, portals, and calendar days change without notice.

---

## Pierce County (Tacoma) — ≈930k

**Pierce County District Court**
- County page: `https://www.piercecountywa.gov/101/District-Court`
- Courthouse: County-City Building, 930 Tacoma Ave S, Tacoma, WA 98402.
- E-filing: **LINX** (Pierce County's Legal Information Network
  eXchange) — start from the county's e-filing page,
  `https://www.piercecountywa.gov/374/E-Filing`, which links to the LINX
  portal; a free LINX account is required. E-filing and e-service are
  **mandatory** for most filers under **PCLGR 30 / PCLR 30**; read that
  rule before your first filing.
- Local rules: Pierce County District Court LCR — from
  `https://www.courts.wa.gov/court_rules/` follow "District Court Rules"
  to the court-by-court list, or use the county site. Check the current
  civil motion docket and confirmation requirements there. (The in-repo
  court-rules corpus has the statewide CR/CRLJ/etc., not Pierce's local
  rules.)
- Records / clerk contact: `PCDCRecords@piercecountywa.gov`.

**Pierce County Superior Court** (general jurisdiction)
- Clerk: Pierce County Clerk, County-City Building, 930 Tacoma Ave S,
  Tacoma, WA 98402. Uses LINX as well; working copies are generally
  required for superior court motions — confirm via PCLR.

## Snohomish County (Everett + branches) — ≈844k

**Snohomish County District Court** — four physical divisions; **file in
the division assigned to the case**:
- **Everett Division** — 3000 Rockefeller Ave, Everett, WA 98201.
- **South Division** — 20520 68th Ave W, Lynnwood, WA 98036.
- **Evergreen Division** — 14414 179th Ave SE, Monroe, WA 98272.
- **Cascade Division** — 415 E Burke Ave, Arlington, WA 98223.
- County page: `https://snohomishcountywa.gov/194/About-the-District-Court-District-Court-`
  (court divisions / locations: `https://snohomishcountywa.gov/524/Court-Divisions`).
- E-filing: Snohomish County e-filing portal — link from the county page.
- Local rules: Snohomish County District Court local rules — see
  `https://www.courts.wa.gov/court_rules/` and the county site for the current civil
  calendar and any confirmation requirement.

**Snohomish County Superior Court**
- Clerk: Snohomish County Clerk, 3000 Rockefeller Ave, Everett, WA 98201.
  Superior court e-filing via the Snohomish County portal; working copies
  generally required (see Snohomish County LCR).

## When you need a court not listed here

This skill covers the most populous counties. For any other Washington
county, go straight to the Washington Courts directory
(`https://www.courts.wa.gov/court_dir/`) for the address and judges, and
to `https://www.courts.wa.gov/court_rules/` → "Superior / District / Municipal Court
Rules" for the court-by-court list of local rules (the in-repo
`wa-law-references/references/court-rules/` corpus has the statewide rule
sets, not the local ones).
The cross-county workflow in `references/filing-and-service.md` still
applies.
