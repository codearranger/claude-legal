# wa-draft-note — Note for Motion Docket

## Prompt
Draft the Note for Motion Docket for my motion to compel. Hearing is
set for May 15, 2026 at 9:00 a.m. in KCDC Burien.

## Expected triggers
- `wa-draft-note`
- `wa-kcdc`

## Acceptance criteria
- Caption matches the motion
- Title: "NOTE FOR MOTION DOCKET"
- Movant identified with correct party designation (pro se)
- **Matter being noted** (e.g., "Defendant's Motion to Compel Further
  Responses to First Discovery")
- **Hearing date, time, division** — Friday, May 15, 2026 at 9:00
  a.m., KCDC Burien Division
- **Judge** — identifies BU2 / Hon. Laumann (if Burien civil)
- **Matter calendar routing**: KCDC.CivilMGT@kingcounty.gov
- Signature block with contact info
- Certificate of Service

## Common failure modes
- Wrong division or courtroom
- Wrong routing address
- Missing judge identification
- No certificate of service
- Incorrect time format (use 9:00 a.m., not 0900 or 9:00AM)
