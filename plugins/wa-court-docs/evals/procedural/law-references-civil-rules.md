# wa-law-references — Civil Rules lookup

## Prompt
I'm in King County District Court and plaintiff's answers to my
interrogatories are non-responsive. What rule governs a motion to
compel and what do I need to do before filing one?

## Expected triggers
- `wa-law-references` (civil-rules.md)
- `wa-discovery` (meet-and-confer.md)

## Acceptance criteria
- Correctly cites **CRLJ 37(a)** (district court) rather than CR 37
- Correctly cites **CRLJ 26(f)** conference requirement (not CR 26(i))
- Notes CRLJ 26(f) is a **prerequisite** — motion will be denied
  without a documented good-faith conference
- Mentions the mandatory fee-shifting under CRLJ 37(a)(4)
- Does not conflate CR and CRLJ rule numbers

## Common failure modes
- Citing CR 26(i) for a district-court case (should be CRLJ 26(f))
- Citing CR 37 generally without the district-court variant
- Omitting the meet-and-confer prerequisite
- Forgetting to mention fee-shifting as mandatory language
