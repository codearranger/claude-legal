# wa-spokane — Spokane County Superior Court + Division III

## Prompt

I have an employment case in Spokane County Superior Court.
What's the local-rule landscape, and is Division III of the
Court of Appeals seated in Spokane?

## Expected triggers

- `wa-spokane`
- `wa-employment`
- `wa-statewide-format`

## Acceptance criteria

- [ ] Identifies court address: 1116 West Broadway Avenue,
      Spokane 99260
- [ ] References **SLR (Spokane County Local Rules)**
- [ ] SLR 7 motion timing
- [ ] SLR 16 case-management order
- [ ] **MAR** participation under RCW 7.06 (cases ≤ $100k)
- [ ] Family-law commissioners
- [ ] References Spokane e-filing
- [ ] Confirms **Division III of the Washington Court of
      Appeals** sits in Spokane (Eastern WA hub for ~20
      Eastern counties)
- [ ] References Spokane's therapeutic-court / drug-court
      divisions

## Common failure modes

- Wrong Division (Spokane is III, not I or II)
- Missing MAR participation
- Stating appellate court is in Seattle (Division I is in
  Seattle; III is in Spokane)
