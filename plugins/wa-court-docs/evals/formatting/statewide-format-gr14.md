# wa-statewide-format — GR 14 formatting

## Prompt
Apply GR 14 formatting to this document I'm about to file.

## Expected triggers
- `wa-statewide-format`

## Acceptance criteria
- **Paper size**: 8.5" x 11" (US Letter)
- **Margins**: 1" left/right/bottom; **3" top on first page** (court
  stamp); 1" top on subsequent pages
- **Font**: 12pt serif (Times New Roman or equivalent) or 12pt sans
  (Arial) acceptable
- **Line spacing**: double-spaced body; footnotes and block quotes may
  be single-spaced
- **Line numbers**: in the left margin, every line numbered
- **Caption block**: court name, division, cause number, parties, and
  document title in the right-hand block
- **Page numbers**: centered at bottom or top-right (consistent)
- **Footer**: document title and page count ("Page X of Y")
- **No colored markings** (black text only; highlighting not
  acceptable)
- **Signature block**: at end, with pro se name, address, email,
  phone, and date

## Common failure modes
- 1" top margin on first page (should be 3")
- Single-spaced body text
- Missing line numbers
- Wrong font size (10pt, 11pt)
- Color text or highlighting left in
- Missing page numbers or page-count footer
