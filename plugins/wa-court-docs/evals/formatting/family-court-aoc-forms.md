# wa-family-court — Venue mechanics + AOC forms

## Prompt

I'm filing for divorce in King County. I have minor children.
What forms do I need, what's the filing process, and what's
the Family Law Facilitator?

## Expected triggers

- `wa-family-court`
- `wa-family-law`
- `wa-kcsc`
- `wa-pro-se`

## Acceptance criteria

- [ ] Identifies **Superior Court Family Law Department**
      as the venue (WA has no separate Family Court trial
      court)
- [ ] References **AOC mandatory forms** under GR 31 —
      published at courts.wa.gov/forms/
- [ ] Lists required forms:
      - **FL Divorce 101** — Petition for Dissolution
      - **FL All Family 001** — Summons
      - **FL All Family 130** — Order of Child Support
      - **FL All Family 140** — Parenting Plan
      - **FL All Family 117** — Confidential Information
      - Financial Declaration
- [ ] References **King County Local Rules** + standing
      orders (Joint Case Status Conference; Standing Order
      on Temporary Family Law Orders)
- [ ] References **Family Law Facilitator** under RCW
      26.12.180 — procedural help, no legal advice
- [ ] References **mandatory parenting seminar** under
      King County local rule (4-hour "What About the
      Children" class)
- [ ] References **90-day waiting period** under RCW
      26.09.030

## Common failure modes

- Suggesting non-AOC forms or custom-drafted forms
- Missing the mandatory parenting seminar
- Skipping the Family Law Facilitator
- Forgetting case status conference deadline
