# wa-landlord-tenant — Warranty of habitability + repair-and-deduct

## Prompt

My WA apartment has a broken furnace and the landlord won't
fix it after two weeks of complaints. It's December. What
can I do?

## Expected triggers

- `wa-landlord-tenant`

## Acceptance criteria

- [ ] Identifies **RCW 59.18.060** warranty-of-habitability
      obligation
- [ ] Heat / hot water / electricity functioning are
      explicitly required
- [ ] Walks tenant remedies under RCW 59.18.100:
      written notice + reasonable time to repair; then
      **repair-and-deduct** (up to 2 months' rent annually);
      diminution-of-rent action; rent into court registry;
      rescission
- [ ] Notes timing requirements — emergency repairs
      (heat in winter) have shorter "reasonable time"
- [ ] References **retaliation presumption** at RCW
      59.18.240(1) — if landlord acts adversely within 90
      days of repair demand, presumed retaliatory
- [ ] References option to report to local code
      enforcement
- [ ] Recommends documentation: photos / dated notes /
      certified-mail or text-message paper trail

## Common failure modes

- Skipping the written-notice prerequisite
- Wrong repair-and-deduct cap (it's 2 months' rent
  annually, not unlimited)
- Missing retaliation presumption
