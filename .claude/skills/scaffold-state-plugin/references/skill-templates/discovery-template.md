A well-drafted RFP is:

- Specific (defined category of documents)
- Tied to a claim or defense
- Bounded by time and party

## Meet and confer

**Required** under most state civil rules and almost every
court's local rules.

> **TODO**: Document the state's meet-and-confer requirement:
> - Statewide civil rule (state's FRCP 37 / ORCP 46 analog)
> - Local-rule specific certifications for the high-volume
>   courts

A good meet-and-confer:

- Specific identification of each disputed item
- Statement of the moving party's position
- Inquiry into the opposing party's position
- Documented attempt to resolve
